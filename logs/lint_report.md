## LLM Audit: Coverage gaps
# Coverage Gaps Audit — Sustainable Travel to the Maldives Wiki

## Key Findings

### 1. **Southern Atolls Severely Underrepresented**
Despite multiple passing references (e.g., "Popular Atolls and Islands", "Resort Pricing by Season"), there is almost no dedicated coverage of the southern atolls beyond single-fact entries for Fuvahmulah Atoll and Addu Atoll Gastronomy.
- **Missing:** Gaafu Alifu, Gaafu Dhaalu, Gnaviyani, Thaa, Meemu atolls
- **Suggested sources:** Visit Maldives regional guide to southern atolls; Addu City tourism board content; Gaafu atolls dive site guides

### 2. **No Visa & Entry Requirements Detail Beyond One Page**
Only the brief "Visa and Entry Requirements" entry exists. No coverage of:
- IMUGA declaration specifics and timing
- Customs prohibitions in detail (religious items, drones, e-cigarettes)
- Departure tax / airport development fee
- **Suggested sources:** Maldives Immigration official site; IMUGA portal documentation; Maldives Customs Service import restrictions page

### 3. **Health, Vaccinations & Medical Infrastructure Missing**
"Health and Well-being" only covers climate adaptation framing. No practical traveler health info.
- **Missing:** Recommended vaccinations, dengue/mosquito-borne illness risk, decompression chamber locations (critical for divers), medical evacuation logistics
- **Suggested sources:** CDC Maldives traveler health page; DAN (Divers Alert Network) Maldives chamber directory; WHO country health profile

### 4. **Travel Insurance Coverage Is a Single Mention**
Only mentioned in passing under "Liveaboard Trip Preparation" and "Packing for the Maldives". No dedicated page.
- **Suggested sources:** DAN dive insurance comparison; World Nomads Maldives guide; dedicated page on evacuation coverage requirements

### 5. **LGBTQ+ and Solo Female Traveler Safety Absent**
"Solo Travel to Maldives" exists as a stub but says nothing about legal context (same-sex relations criminalized), practical safety, or discretion on local islands. No coverage of solo female considerations on inhabited islands.
- **Suggested sources:** Equaldex Maldives entry; Journeywoman solo female Maldives guide; ILGA country report

### 6. **Ramadan Travel Impact Only Briefly Noted**
Mentioned once in "Best Time to Visit by Month" (May). No dedicated page on how Ramadan affects local island dining hours, ferry schedules, café availability, or resort vs. local-island experience differences.
- **Suggested sources:** Visit Maldives Ramadan advisory; guesthouse association guidance on Ramadan operations

### 7. **Carbon Footprint of Maldives Travel — Major Sustainability Gap**
Given the wiki's sustainability focus, it is striking that there is **no coverage of aviation emissions**, carbon offsetting, or the sustainability paradox of long-haul + seaplane travel to a climate-vulnerable nation.
- **Suggested sources:** Sustainable Travel International offset programs; ICAO carbon calculator methodology; academic paper on tourism emissions in SIDS (Small Island Developing States)
