# Atoll Transfer FAQ

## Overview
This FAQ explains how private transfer booking works in the Maldives and why advance booking is important.

## Key points
- Transfers by domestic plane, seaplane, speedboat, and ferry are often fully booked on the day of arrival.
- Flight details are required for proper transfer scheduling.
- There are no public resort ferries; resort transfers are private or arranged through operators.
- Seaplane transfers run only during daylight hours, and same-day seaplane connections require arriving in Malé before mid-afternoon.
- Late flight arrival or missed transfer may mean no refund unless the service provider cancels.

This page is especially useful for transfer timing, baggage, and booking assumptions, even if some of the wording reflects an older operational snapshot.
