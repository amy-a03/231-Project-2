# A dozen ways to live like a local

## Overview
This Atolls story page offers a budget-friendly, local-life perspective on the Maldives rather than a resort-only framing.

## Food and daily life
The page includes hedhikaa, eating like a local, learning to cook island specialties, drinking fresh coconut, and eating freshly caught fish. It also references tea shops, local food culture, and everyday island routines.

## Beyond food
Alongside food, the article suggests local-style activities such as sandbank trips, football with locals, bicycle rides, sunrise yoga, sunset cruises with dolphins, and trying local handiwork.

## Why it matters
This is a good cultural companion to the other food pages because it shows how Maldivian dining is part of a broader rhythm of everyday island life.
