# Authentic Addu Flavours

## Overview
The Addu gastronomy page focuses on the southern Maldivian food culture and describes it as a blend of South Indian, Arabian, and Sri Lankan influences.

## Local dishes
It highlights Addu Reha Curry, Theleli Folhi, Addu Havaadhu, and Addu Bondi as signature foods. The page also notes the value of coconut-based curries, sweet and savory snacks, and a king coconut from a local Gaadiya.

## Practical food culture
The page is also useful for understanding how food is prepared and enjoyed in the south, including the idea of eating by hand and pairing meals with tea.

## Why it matters
Addu is one of the best entry points for food culture that feels distinctly local, regional, and less resort-shaped. This page is especially helpful if you want a southern Maldivian angle on cuisine.
