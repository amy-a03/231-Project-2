# Travel to Ari Atoll

## Overview
This Ari Atoll connectivity page explains how travelers can reach the atoll using several different transport modes depending on pace, comfort, and budget.

## Transport options
- Speedboats, including private and shared options, are available from the airport.
- Public ferry dhonis run from Malé City to islands in Ari Atoll on a weekly schedule.
- Domestic flights connect Velana International Airport to Villa International Airport Maamigili.
- Seaplanes operate from Velana International Airport to Ari Atoll resorts.

This is a useful route-specific page for understanding how one atoll combines ferry, domestic flight, speedboat, and seaplane access.
