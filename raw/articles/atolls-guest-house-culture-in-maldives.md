# The Guest House culture in Maldives

## Overview
This page explains how guesthouses expanded after tourism rules changed and how they opened up more local-style travel across inhabited islands.

## Food and local life
While the page is broader than food alone, it ties guesthouse stays to everyday Maldivian life, including tea shops, hedhikaa, local community interaction, and the chance to experience the islands more like a resident than a resort guest.

## Accommodation context
It positions guesthouses as part of a wider set of accommodation types that also includes resorts, hotels, and liveaboards.
