# Best Time to Visit the Maldives

Source page: [Audley Travel US - Best time to visit the Maldives](https://www.audleytravel.com/us/the-maldives/best-time-to-visit?utm_source=chatgpt.com#apr)

## Overview

Audley Travel identifies January through April as the best period to visit the Maldives because it falls in the dry season. During this time, visitors can expect warm tropical weather, low humidity, and relatively little rainfall. Average daytime highs are generally consistent throughout the year, around 84°F to 88°F.

May through December remains warm, with average lows rarely below 75°F, but rain and storms become more likely. Even in the wetter months, the page notes that long bright spells are common. The wet season can also offer better value because lower prices and travel offers are more common outside the peak travel period.

## Month-by-Month Travel Notes

### January

January is described as a strong month for a beach vacation, with plenty of warmth and sunshine. Diving and snorkeling conditions are favorable because underwater visibility is good. This is a popular travel month, so advance booking is recommended.

### February to March

February and March are the driest months. Conditions are warm, humidity is low, and visibility for diving and snorkeling is very good. These months are also highly popular, so travelers should book early.

### April

April is still a good month to travel, with sunshine and good water visibility. Rain risk rises slightly, especially later in the month.

### May to September

May through September remains warm and can still bring long sunny periods, but rain and storms are more likely. Plankton in the water can reduce diving visibility. The page frames this as a good-value period because lower prices and offers are more common. August remains a popular month despite falling in the wetter season.

### October to November

October and November combine warm temperatures and sunny periods with a higher chance of rain and storms. These months are highlighted as a good time to see whale sharks and manta rays, which are drawn by plankton. Travel offers are often available, making this a value-oriented period.

### December

December is warm and can still have extended sunny periods, though rain and storms are more likely. Hotels often maintain good offers until shortly before the Christmas peak, so early December can be a better-value time to travel.

## Practical Takeaways

- Best overall weather window: January to April.
- Driest months: February and March.
- Strong diving and snorkeling visibility: January through April, especially February and March.
- Better-value travel periods: May to early December, especially before the Christmas peak.
- Increased rain and storm risk: May through December.
- Wildlife note: October and November are favorable for seeing whale sharks and manta rays because plankton attracts them.
