# Sustainability

Source page: [Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru Sustainability](https://www.banyantree.com/maldives/vabbinfaru/sustainability)

## Overview

Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru presents sustainability as a core brand value. The resort says it uses an integrated and participatory approach to minimize impact while safeguarding human and physical environments for present and future generations.

## Certification

The page states that EarthCheck awarded Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru Gold Certification in 2022. It says the property has had continuous certification since 2012, with annual measurement of indicators such as:

- Energy.
- Emissions.
- Water.
- Waste.
- Paper use.
- Chemical and pesticide use.
- Community involvement.

## Reef Restoration

The resort describes a reef restoration program that uses coral fragments and electric reefs to help damaged reefs recover and increase resilience. It notes:

- The first electric reef in the Maldives was built in 1996 at Dhawa Ihuru.
- A second followed in 1998.
- A third was created on the house reef of Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru in 2001.
- In 2016, 1,000 coral fragments were transplanted onto each coral rope nursery.

## Plastic Reduction

The page says Banyan Tree began reducing single-use plastic in 2018 and that Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru and Dhawa Ihuru achieved a 75% reduction in total single-use plastic usage.
