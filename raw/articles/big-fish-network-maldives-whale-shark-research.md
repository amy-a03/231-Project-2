# Big Fish Network

Source page: [Big Fish Network](https://maldiveswhalesharkresearch.org/bigfishnetwork/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

## Overview

The Big Fish Network is a citizen-science platform built by the founders of the Maldives Whale Shark Research Programme to help document whale shark encounters and build stewardship among tourism operators.

## What the Network Does

The platform lets guides and operators:

- Submit whale shark sightings.
- Upload encounter photos.
- Access identification archives.
- Track individual shark life histories.
- Share sighting updates and reports.

## Why It Matters

The programme says the network helps generate consistent, high-quality data while also building communication and responsibility among operators.

## Practical Features Mentioned

The page describes:

- Training workshops for guides.
- An online portal for sightings.
- A mobile app for members and guests.
- Leaderboards and report posters for operators.
