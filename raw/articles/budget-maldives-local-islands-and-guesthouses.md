# The Ultimate Guide to Maldives Local Islands and Guest Houses

Source page: [Budget Maldives - The Ultimate Guide to Maldives Local Islands and Guest Houses](https://www.budgetmaldives.com/maldives-local-islands/)

## Overview

Budget Maldives presents local islands as the heart of affordable Maldives travel. The page frames them as a way to experience authenticity, local food, community life, and guesthouses that offer real value compared with private resort islands.

## Why Stay on a Local Island

The guide says local islands offer:

- Authentic Maldivian life.
- Lower accommodation costs.
- Access to real local cuisine.
- Community interactions.
- A slower, more personal travel pace.

It also notes that local island hotels and guesthouses can be discounted by up to 60%.

## Island Highlights

### Gulhi

Small, peaceful, and close to Malé, with quiet beaches and good snorkeling.

### Guraidhoo

Known for surf culture, local cafés, and budget-friendly guesthouses.

### Maafushi

The main local tourism hub, with lots of guesthouses and day trips.

### Thulusdhoo

Popular with surfers and travelers who want an artsier island vibe.

### Dhigurah

Long beaches, snorkeling, manta rays, and whale sharks nearby.

### Omadhoo

Small and peaceful, with a slower pace and homely guesthouses.

### Thoddoo

Known for farms, fresh produce, and a greener island feel.

### Fulidhoo

A traditional, low-key island with affordable stays and quiet beaches.

### Fuvahmulah

A distinctive single-island atoll with freshwater lakes and unique landscapes.

## Choosing an Island

The guide suggests matching the island to the traveler's priorities:

- Tranquility: Dhigurah, Omadhoo, Ukulhas.
- Budget travel: Maafushi, Gulhi, Thulusdhoo.
- Surfing and adventure: Thulusdhoo, Guraidhoo.
- Authentic culture: Feridhoo, Thoddoo, Ukulhas.
- Unique landscapes: Fuvahmulah.

## Practical Booking Tips

- Book early for the best seasonal deals.
- Plan transfers in advance.
- Check amenities carefully.
- Respect local customs on attire and alcohol.
- Consider a multi-island itinerary.
