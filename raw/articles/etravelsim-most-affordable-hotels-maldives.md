# The Most Affordable Hotels in Maldives

Requested source page: [ETravelSim - The Most Affordable Hotels in Maldives](https://etravelsim.com/blogs/updates/the-most-affordable-hotels-in-maldives?srsltid=AfmBOopIf1VzCO3bmQpZqRMFM91Zeav58xKLeOsTbpgK4cE_WW5uaFOh)

## Overview

ETravelSim frames the Maldives as more affordable than its stereotype suggests. The article argues that local-island guesthouses and smart transfer choices can make the destination accessible without resort-level budgets.

## Main Budget Strategy

The article's core point is that travelers can choose between:

- Private island resorts.
- Inhabited local islands with guesthouses.

It says local-island guesthouses can cost around $60 a night, compared with much higher resort prices.

## How to Save

The article recommends:

- Staying on local islands.
- Eating at local cafes.
- Using speedboat transfers instead of ferries where timing matters.
- Traveling in shoulder months, especially May and October.

The page says shoulder months can reduce hotel prices significantly.

## Featured Affordable Stays

The article highlights these guesthouse-style options:

- Kaani Village, Maafushi, around $70/night.
- Season Paradise, Thulusdhoo, around $90/night.
- Boutique Beach, Dhigurah, around $120/night.

It also frames Embudu Village, Sun Island, and Reethi Beach as lower-cost resort choices for travelers who want a taste of overwater lodging without the full luxury price tag.

## Local Island Notes

The article reminds travelers that local islands have:

- Modest dress expectations in town.
- Alcohol restrictions.
- Designated bikini beaches for tourists.

It also emphasizes that local-island guesthouses still provide air conditioning and modern comfort.

## Budget Planning

The article gives a sample seven-day budget:

- Accommodation: $500.
- Food and dining: $350.
- Transfers: $80.
- Miscellaneous: $40.
- Total: $970.

## FAQ Highlights

- Best budget months: May and October.
- Affordable dining: local cafes and Hedhikaa.
- Overwater bungalows on a budget: possible through split stays and entry-level luxury resorts.
