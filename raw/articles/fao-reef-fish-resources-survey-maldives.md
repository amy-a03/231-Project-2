# Reef Fish Resources Survey in the Maldives

Source page: [FAO - Reef Fish Resources Survey in the Maldives](https://www.fao.org/4/ae443e/ae443e00.htm)

## Overview

This FAO working paper reports on exploratory fishing trials in North Malé Atoll and evaluates the potential of reef fish as a resource in the Maldives.

## Why It Was Written

The paper was created to assess the status of reef fish resources, the efficiency of fishing gears, and the prospects for developing a viable reef fish fishery.

## Key Findings

The report says:

- Reef fish were already part of local consumption and export activity.
- Gear, catch composition, and fishing intensity were studied.
- The survey aimed to understand abundance and exploitation rates.

## Relevance to Tourism

The paper predates the modern tourism boom but is important for understanding how reef fish resources sit alongside tourism development and local economic pressures.
