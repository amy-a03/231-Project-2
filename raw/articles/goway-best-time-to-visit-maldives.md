# Best Time to Visit Maldives

Source page: [Goway Travel - Best Time to Visit Maldives](https://www.goway.com/destinations/indian-ocean/maldives/best-time-to-visit?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

## Overview

Goway describes the Maldives as pleasant for most of the year, with wetter weather during the monsoon from June through August. The page recommends packing swimwear year-round and notes that a sarong can be useful as a beach blanket or extra layer. Because the Maldives receives strong equatorial sun, Goway recommends SPF 30+ sunscreen.

## Ideal Time to Visit

One of the best periods to visit is November through April. Weather is warm and dry, with average temperatures around 86°F / 30°C, little rain, and low humidity.

## Peak Tourist Season

January through April are the driest months and the peak resort season. Travelers should expect:

- Clear skies.
- Full availability of activities.
- Strong photo conditions.
- More tourists.
- Higher prices.

## Most Affordable Time

May through November is the best value period. Goway notes that resorts may offer lower room rates, free transfers, upgrades, and free nights during this wetter season.

Travelers should expect sudden downpours, though they are often short. The page specifically suggests May or September-October as windows for travelers hoping to avoid the wettest months of the season.

## Recommended Trip Length

Goway recommends one week to ten days in the Maldives. This allows enough time to experience variety, including island hopping.
