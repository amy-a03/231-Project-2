# The Complete Maldives Travel Guide

## Overview
This guide frames the Maldives as a destination for atmosphere as much as activity, while still laying out the key practical basics for trip planning.

## Practical basics
- Visa on arrival is available for most nationalities for 30 days.
- The IMUGA traveler declaration must be completed online before arrival.
- Alcohol is available on resort islands and liveaboards, but not on local islands.
- Standard holiday clothing is fine at resorts, while local islands expect modest dress with bikinis restricted to designated beaches.
- US dollars are accepted widely, though the Maldivian rufiyaa remains the local currency.
- Alcohol, pork products, and certain religious items are restricted for import.

## Getting around
The guide explains that international flights arrive at Velana International Airport, with onward transfers by speedboat or seaplane depending on distance from Malé. It also notes that some later arrivals may require an overnight stay before onward transfer the next day.

This is a broad reference page for travel logistics, cultural expectations, and packing or transfer decisions. It is useful as a general backbone source for a Maldives knowledge base.
