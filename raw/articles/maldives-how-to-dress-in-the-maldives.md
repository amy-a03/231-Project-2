# How to Dress in the Maldives

## Core rule
Dress expectations depend on where you are. Resort islands allow normal beachwear and swimwear, while local islands expect modest public clothing, with bikinis limited to designated bikini beaches.

## Resort islands
At resort islands, casual beach clothing is acceptable, including swimwear, shorts, and tank tops. For restaurants, the page recommends smart casual or casual wear rather than swimwear or gym clothing.

## Local islands
On local islands, public clothing should be unrevealing and respectful. Swimwear should be kept to designated beach areas. The article advises covering shoulders and knees in public spaces and dressing conservatively for mosques.

## Traditional clothing
The article also notes that wearing traditional Maldivian clothing, such as Feyli and Libaas, is welcomed and appreciated by locals.

## Packing takeaway
For mixed resort and local-island trips, pack lightweight layers, cover-ups, and modest clothing options so you can move comfortably between settings without breaking local norms.
