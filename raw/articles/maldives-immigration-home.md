# Maldives Immigration Home

## Overview
The Maldives Immigration homepage is the main entry point for immigration services and current border-management information. It is not a detailed visa-rule page, but it points travelers toward the official tourist visa and IMUGA resources.

## Current homepage signals
The site highlights current immigration statistics, including total arrivals and tourist arrivals, and identifies the Controller General of Immigration.

## What travelers should use it for
For entry requirements, the homepage directs travelers to the official tourist visa page and to IMUGA for the Traveller Declaration. That is the place to check the live rules before travel.

## Why it matters
This page is useful as a verified official starting point, but the detailed visa conditions live on the dedicated visa pages rather than the homepage itself.
