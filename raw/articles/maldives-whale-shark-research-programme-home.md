## Overview

The Maldives Whale Shark Research Programme is a conservation charity and research group focused on whale sharks in the Maldives and the greater Indian Ocean. Its work combines research, community mobilisation, and citizen science.

## Core Mission

The programme exists to:

- Study whale shark population dynamics.
- Understand habitat use and movement.
- Support conservation policy.
- Involve tourism operators and local communities.

## Why It Matters

The Maldives is one of the world's best-known whale shark destinations, and the programme plays a central role in documenting individual sharks, informing management, and promoting responsible encounters.
