# Manta Trust

Source page: [Manta Trust](https://www.mantatrust.org/)

## Overview

Manta Trust describes itself as a conservation organization focused on research, education, and collaboration to protect manta rays and devil rays.

## Core Mission

The organization says its vision is a sustainable ocean future where manta rays and their relatives thrive in healthy marine ecosystems.

## Scale of the Issue

The page notes that there are 10 species of manta and devil rays worldwide and that they are threatened primarily by fisheries.

## What the Organization Does

Manta Trust says it works through:

- Global affiliate networks.
- Research.
- Education.
- Policy support.
- Conservation collaboration.

## Maldives Relevance

The homepage includes Maldives imagery and reflects the organization's long-running role in Maldivian manta research and tourism guidance.
