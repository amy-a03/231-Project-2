# When to Visit Maldives: Peak vs Off vs Shoulder Season

Source page: [Oaga Resorts - When to Visit Maldives](https://oagaresorts.com/journal/best-season-to-travel/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

## Overview

Oaga Resorts frames the Maldives as an all-year destination but argues that each season offers different tradeoffs for weather, crowds, and price. The page is organized around peak season, off-season, and shoulder season.

## Peak Season

Peak season runs from December to February. Oaga describes this as the best fit for travelers who want sunshine, blue skies, gentle sea breezes, and a lively atmosphere.

Pros:

- Ideal weather.
- Great conditions for snorkeling, scuba diving, and surfing.
- Bustling atmosphere.

Cons:

- Higher prices.
- Larger crowds.

## Off-Season

Off-season runs from May to November. The guide says this is ideal for travelers who prefer serenity, lower rates, and a deeper connection with nature.

Pros:

- Lower accommodation and flight prices.
- More privacy.
- Rich marine life.
- Good snorkeling and diving.

Cons:

- Higher humidity.
- Some activities may be limited.

## Shoulder Season

The shoulder season spans March and April. Oaga presents it as the best of both worlds, with pleasant weather, fewer crowds, and a balance between peak and off-season conditions.

Pros:

- Tranquil atmosphere.
- Warm temperatures.
- Occasional rain.
- Balanced experience.

Cons:

- Slightly higher prices than off-season.

## FAQ Highlights

- Off-season travel offers lower rates and more privacy.
- The shoulder season is the transitional period between peak and off-season.
- Travelers should choose based on whether they value weather, crowds, or price most.

## Practical Takeaways

- Best weather and liveliest atmosphere: December through February.
- Best value and privacy: May through November.
- Best balance: March and April.
