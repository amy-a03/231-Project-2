# Patterns of change in coral reef communities of a remote Maldivian atoll revisited after eleven years

Source page: [PMC article](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10710173/)

## Overview

This paper examines how coral reef communities changed over eleven years at a remote Maldivian atoll, using a long-term ecological comparison.

## Main Themes

The accessible snippet and references show that the paper addresses:

- Coral cover change.

- Reef-community recovery.

The abstract and references tie the Maldives to repeated thermal anomalies, bleaching events, and localized pressures such as tourism and dredging.
