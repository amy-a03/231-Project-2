# Answers to Your Top 10 Maldives Travel Questions

## Overview
This FAQ answers common visitor questions around transfers, beach access, local-island etiquette, alcohol, ferry logistics, and what to expect on a Maldives trip.

## Key practical points
- Resort transfers are usually arranged by the property or travel operator.
- Public ferries serve local islands, not resorts.
- Local islands and resort islands have different rules around swimwear and alcohol.
- Bikini beaches exist on many local islands, but swimwear should stay within those designated areas.
- Domestic flight and speedboat timing can change with weather and operational conditions.
- The sea of stars is a natural bioluminescent phenomenon and cannot be guaranteed.

## Cultural and dress guidance
The page reinforces that Maldives is a Muslim country and that modest behavior and clothing are expected outside resort islands. It also notes that respectful, lightweight clothing is appropriate when visiting communities.

## Packing and entry notes
The article points visitors toward essentials like lightweight clothing, swimwear for water activities, reef-friendly sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and awareness of restricted imports such as alcohol and pork products.
