# Soneva

Source page: [World Sustainable Hospitality Alliance - Soneva](https://sustainablehospitalityalliance.org/members/soneva/)

## Overview

The Sustainable Hospitality Alliance presents Soneva as a world-leading sustainable luxury resort operator founded in 1995. The profile describes Soneva as built on the idea that a business should have a purpose beyond shareholder returns.

## Maldives Presence

The page states that Soneva includes:

- Soneva Fushi.
- Soneva Jani.
- Soneva Secret.

It also notes Soneva Kiri in Thailand as part of the wider portfolio.

## Sustainability Reporting

The profile points to Soneva's Total Impact Assessment, which measures direct and indirect social and environmental impacts, including supply chain and guest travel.

## Sustainability Initiatives

The Alliance page highlights Soneva's sustainability work across areas such as:

- Air quality.
- Biodiversity.
- Youth.
- Zero waste.

## Coral Restoration

The profile says Soneva is a driver of net-positive hospitality and that the Soneva Foundation Coral Restoration programme aims to outplant 150,000 coral fragments each year to help restore reef systems in the Maldives.
