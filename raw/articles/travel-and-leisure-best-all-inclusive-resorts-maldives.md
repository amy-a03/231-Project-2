# The 9 Best All-inclusive Resorts in the Maldives

Source page: [Travel + Leisure - Best Maldives All-inclusive Resorts](https://www.travelandleisure.com/hotels-resorts/all-inclusive-resorts/best-maldives-all-inclusive-resorts)

## Overview

Travel + Leisure recommends all-inclusive properties as a strong way to simplify a Maldives trip after the long journey to Malé. The list focuses on editor-loved, award-winning resorts that bundle meals, drinks, activities, and in some cases transfers or spa perks.

## Resorts on the List

The page names nine resorts:

- The Ritz-Carlton Maldives, Fari Islands
- Park Hyatt Maldives Hadahaa
- Komandoo Maldives Island Resort
- Alila Kothaifaru Maldives
- Kudadoo Maldives
- Soneva Fushi
- Outrigger Maldives Maafushivaru
- Soneva Jani
- Conrad Maldives Rangali Island

## Luxury and Eco Highlights

The guide draws a clear distinction between romantic, activity-focused, and sustainability-focused properties.

Notable examples:

- Kudadoo Maldives is described as the country's first entirely solar-powered property.
- Soneva Fushi is praised for climate-conscious operations and generous all-inclusive perks.
- Park Hyatt Maldives Hadahaa is known for a 360-degree house reef.
- Alila Kothaifaru Maldives emphasizes classes, spa treatments, and dolphin cruises.
- Outrigger Maldives Maafushivaru offers whale shark and manta ray spotting.
- Conrad Maldives Rangali Island combines an underwater restaurant and suite with family-friendly and adults-only areas.

## Best-Fit Themes

The article's standout amenity calls include:

- Private pool villas.
- Butler service.
- Dining islands.
- Candlelit beach dinners.
- Underwater dining.
- Waterslides.
- Wellness centers.
- Marine wildlife encounters.

## Know Before You Go

The page advises:

- Consider splitting time across more than one island if you want variety.
- Avoid packing prohibited items, including alcohol and pork products.
- Pack reef-safe sunscreen because equatorial UV is strong.
