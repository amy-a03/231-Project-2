## What Reviewers Emphasize

Common themes in the visible review snippets include:

- Affordable stays that still feel relaxing.
- Good snorkeling and coral access.
- Budget-friendly family or couple trips.
- Reasonable service and welcoming staff.
- Good value when packages include meals, spa credits, or boat trips.

## Practical Use

The ranking is useful for comparing budget and value properties side by side, especially for travelers who still want resort-style amenities but at lower price points than ultra-luxury Maldives stays.
