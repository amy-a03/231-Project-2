# Connectivity

## Overview
This page focuses on how the Maldives is connected to the rest of the world and how travelers move within the country after arrival.

## International arrival
The main gateway is Velana International Airport. Visit Maldives notes regular flights from Europe, the Middle East, and South and Southeast Asia, with especially strong traffic from Sri Lanka, India, and Dubai.

## Domestic movement
Once in-country, travelers can use the domestic and international airports across the atolls, as well as scheduled ferries from Malé to many atolls.

## Resort transfers
For resort stays, transfers are typically arranged in advance. Resorts close to the airport tend to use speedboats, while farther resorts are usually reached by seaplane.

## Why it matters
This page is the best broad overview of Maldives transport connectivity, especially for mapping the usual split between speedboat, seaplane, domestic flight, and ferry travel.
