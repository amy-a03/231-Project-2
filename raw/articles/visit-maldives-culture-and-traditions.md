# Visit Maldives: Culture

## Overview
The Maldives is home to a distinct culture and set of traditions shaped by island life and the surrounding sea. The culture has also been influenced by communities and trade across the Indian Ocean.

## Language and identity
Dhivehi is the national language. Its script, Thaana, is unique and developed from Arabic numerals around the 16th century.

## Traditional life
The page highlights the dhoni, the traditional Maldivian boat, as a craft refined over centuries to suit local sea conditions. It also notes a cuisine based heavily on fish and coconut.

## Arts and crafts
Maldivian music and dance reflect influences from East Africa, Arabia, and the Indian subcontinent. The country also has a strong craft tradition, including lacquered wooden ornaments, woven reed mats, and coral carvings.
