# Encounters with the Majestic Manta Rays 101

Source page: [Visit Maldives - Encounters with the Majestic Manta Rays 101](https://visitmaldives.com/en/experience/encounters-with-the-majestic-manta-rays-101)

## Overview

Visit Maldives says the Maldives is home to the largest recorded reef manta ray population in the world, based on fifteen years of data collection by the Manta Trust.

## Seasonal Patterns

The article explains that manta rays migrate across the archipelago according to shifting monsoons and zooplankton distribution. Hanifaru Bay in Baa Atoll is highlighted as a major feeding aggregation site.

## Species Notes

The page identifies the reef manta ray as the common Maldives species and notes that the country also sees seasonal visits from oceanic manta rays.

## Sustainable Encounters

The article gives simple code-of-conduct advice:

- Enter the water calmly.
- Keep a respectful distance.
- Approach from the side.
- Do not chase or touch mantas.
- Respect cleaning stations.

## Conservation Contribution

It says visitors can help by learning about manta ecology and submitting photographs to Manta Trust's ID databases.
