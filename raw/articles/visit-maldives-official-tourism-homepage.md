# Visit Maldives Official Tourism Homepage

Source page: [Visit Maldives - The Sunny Side of Life](https://visitmaldives.com/en)

## Overview

Visit Maldives is the official tourism website for the Maldives. The homepage presents the Maldives under the tourism slogan "The Sunny Side of Life" and highlights the destination's global tourism awards, travel experiences, accommodation categories, official news, and discovery resources.

The homepage highlights the Maldives as:

- The World's Leading Destination for 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025.

## Stay Categories

### Resorts

The page describes Maldives resorts as ranging from club-like resorts to chic boutique resorts, including options for family fun and other resort styles.

### Hotels

Hotels in Male are presented as convenient for business visitors. The page notes that a range of hotels is available.

### Liveaboards

Liveaboards are described as a way to see more of the Maldives. The page notes that liveaboards can focus on activities such as surfing.

### Guesthouses

Guesthouses are presented as a different experience from resort stays because travelers stay in a local town and can experience more local life.

## Discover Maldives Sections

### Geography

The Maldives is described as an Indian Ocean island nation spanning the equator. The homepage states that the country has 1,192 islands stretching across 871 kilometers.

### People

The people section says that while little is known about the ancient history of the Maldives, scholars and historians consider it an old nation populated for more than 2,500 years.

### Culture

The culture section notes that the Maldives has a unique culture and traditions despite a population of just over half a million people. It also notes influences from cultures around the Indian Ocean.

### Environment

The environment section emphasizes the fragility of the Maldivian environment. Coral reefs are described as the foundation of the islands and their natural defense system.
