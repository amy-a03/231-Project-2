# Radisson Blu Resort Maldives

## Overview
This resort page is useful primarily for transport context because it states exactly how the property is reached from Malé and how far it is from the airport.

## Transfer options
Radisson Blu Resort Maldives is about 105 km from Velana International Airport. The page says guests can take a 30-minute seaplane directly to the resort or a 20-minute domestic flight to Maamigili Airport followed by a 15-minute speedboat ride.

## Why it matters
This is a good concrete example of how a single resort can offer both seaplane and domestic-flight-plus-speedboat access, which is a common Maldives planning question.
