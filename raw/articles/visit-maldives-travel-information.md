# Travel Information

## Overview
Visit Maldives presents the country as a place where transport is tied closely to geography. The page covers the core logistics travelers need, including time zone, currency, visa, taxes, weather, clothing, and transfers.

## Getting around
The main transfer pattern is simple: speedboats are the norm for resorts close to Velana International Airport, while seaplanes and domestic flights serve farther destinations. Scheduled ferries also run from Malé to many atolls. If you are traveling to Malé itself, a taxi is available from the airport.

## Practical notes
The page also notes that most visitors arrive through Velana International Airport, that pre-arranged resort transfers are common, and that modest clothing is expected on inhabited islands.
