# Ari Atoll

## Summary
Ari Atoll is a major destination in the Maldives accessible by multiple transport modes including seaplanes, speedboats, domestic flights, and ferries. The atoll offers diverse connectivity options catering to different budgets and travel preferences.

## Key Facts
- Speedboats (private and shared) operate from the airport to Ari Atoll
- Public ferry dhonis run weekly from Malé City to islands in Ari Atoll
- Domestic flights connect Velana International Airport to Villa International Airport Maamigili
- Seaplanes operate from Velana International Airport directly to Ari Atoll resorts
- Multiple transport options allow travelers to choose based on pace, comfort, and budget
- Major atoll region with substantial resort concentration
- Features luxury resort properties including LUX* South Ari Atoll
- One of the most visited atolls in the archipelago
- Significant Booking.com inventory
