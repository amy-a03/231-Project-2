# Dhiffushi Island

## Summary
Dhiffushi is a local island offering budget-friendly accommodation with easy access from Malé and more affordable pricing compared to resort islands. It serves as an accessible alternative for budget travelers.

## Key Facts
- Dhiffushi is easy to reach from the main airport area
- The island offers more affordable accommodation than resort options
- Located near Malé making ferry access convenient
- Suitable for budget travelers seeking local island experiences
