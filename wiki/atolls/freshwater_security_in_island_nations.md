# Freshwater Security in Island Nations

## Summary
The Maldives faces critical freshwater vulnerability due to limited freshwater lenses in coral atolls, with saltwater intrusion and rising sea levels threatening drinking water supplies and agriculture.

## Key Facts
- Freshwater lenses are limited in coral atoll environments
- Saltwater intrusion from sea-level rise threatens freshwater supplies
- Over 80% of land below 1 meter elevation increases saltwater contamination risk
- Freshwater security is essential for both residential and agricultural sectors
- Adaptation requires integrated water management and desalination infrastructure
