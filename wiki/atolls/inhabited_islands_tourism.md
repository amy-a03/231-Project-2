# Inhabited Islands Tourism

## Summary
Inhabited islands in the Maldives have become accessible to tourists through guest house accommodation and regulatory changes, allowing visitors to experience authentic island community life and local dining rather than staying in isolated resorts.

## Key Facts
- Tourism regulations were liberalized to permit guest house accommodations on inhabited islands
- Inhabited islands offer daily interaction with local Maldivian communities
- Access to local tea shops and traditional food experiences
- Provides alternative to isolated resort-based tourism model
