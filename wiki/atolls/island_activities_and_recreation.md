# Island Activities and Recreation

## Summary
A variety of cultural and recreational activities available to visitors seeking to engage authentically with island communities, ranging from water-based experiences to social and physical activities.

## Key Facts
- Sandbank trips are popular local excursions
- Football games provide opportunities to interact with local residents
- Bicycle rides allow exploration of island terrain and communities
- Sunrise yoga is part of island wellness routines
- Sunset dolphin cruises are memorable wildlife experiences
- Traditional local handiwork crafts offer cultural learning opportunities
