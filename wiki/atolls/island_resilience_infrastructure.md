# Island Resilience Infrastructure

## Summary
Infrastructure resilience is a core adaptation priority for the Maldives, requiring climate-proofing of essential systems to withstand sea-level rise, coastal erosion, and other climate hazards across inhabited islands.

## Key Facts
- Infrastructure resilience is a designated adaptation priority
- 358 inhabited islands require climate-resilient infrastructure
- Safer Island Strategy guides infrastructure adaptation
- Coastal protection and erosion management are critical infrastructure needs
