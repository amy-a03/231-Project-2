# Island Types and Access in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives consists of resort islands and local islands with different accessibility rules, amenities, and cultural expectations. Resort islands are privately operated and serve tourists exclusively, while local islands are inhabited communities with their own regulations.

## Key Facts
- Resort islands are not served by public ferries; transfers are arranged privately by the property or travel operator
- Public ferries serve local islands, not resort islands
- Local islands and resort islands have different rules regarding swimwear and alcohol consumption
- Bikini beaches exist on many local islands but swimwear should remain in designated areas
- Domestic flight and speedboat timing can change with weather and operational conditions
