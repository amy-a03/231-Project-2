# Kaafu Atoll

## Summary
Kaafu Atoll is a major region in the Maldives with substantial resort inventory and booking concentration. It encompasses the capital Malé and surrounding popular tourist destinations.

## Key Facts
- One of the primary atoll regions in Booking.com's Maldives directory
- Contains Malé, the capital city of the Maldives
- Major hub for resort bookings and tourism infrastructure
- Includes nearby islands like Hulhumale
