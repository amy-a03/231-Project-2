# Laamu Atoll

## Summary
Laamu Atoll in the southern Maldives is designated as a Hope Spot and home to Six Senses Laamu resort. The atoll is surrounded by Marine Protected Areas and serves as a hub for marine conservation research and sustainable tourism.

## Key Facts
- Located in the southern Maldives
- Designated as the only atoll-level Hope Spot in the Maldives
- Home to Six Senses Laamu sustainable resort
- Surrounded by Marine Protected Areas
- Supports significant marine megafauna populations including manta rays, whale sharks, and sea turtles
