# Local Island Communities

## Summary
Local islands in the Maldives are working communities with active fishing industries, schools, and daily life that contrasts with the isolated resort experience, providing travelers with cultural immersion.

## Key Facts
- Feature active fishing operations with boats departing early
- Have functioning schools and local services
- Contain daily cafés and community spaces
- Maintain cultural and religious practices including alcohol prohibition and modest dress codes
- Public beaches rather than resort-controlled access
- Offer opportunities for travelers to experience authentic Maldivian culture
