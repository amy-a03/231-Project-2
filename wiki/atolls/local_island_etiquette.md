# Local Island Etiquette

## Summary
Visitors to local islands in the Maldives should be aware of cultural norms and behavioral expectations that differ significantly from resort island rules, particularly regarding alcohol, swimwear, and public conduct.

## Key Facts
- Alcohol rules differ between resort islands and local islands
- Local islands have specific regulations about swimwear in public areas
- Respectful behavior and modest dress are essential when visiting local communities
- Bikini beaches are designated areas on some local islands
- Public ferries connect local islands for inter-island travel
