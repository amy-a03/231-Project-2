# Local Island Travel in Maldives

## Summary
Local islands represent the most budget-friendly way to experience the Maldives, offering guesthouse accommodation, authentic Maldivian culture, and access to natural attractions at a fraction of resort costs. These islands have distinct rules and character compared to resort islands.

## Key Facts
- Local island stays cost significantly less than resort accommodation
- Islands like Maafushi and Dhiffushi provide authentic Maldivian experiences
- Ferry access from Malé is available to multiple local islands
- Local islands have stricter cultural and religious guidelines than resorts
- Day trips from local islands to natural attractions are common budget activities
