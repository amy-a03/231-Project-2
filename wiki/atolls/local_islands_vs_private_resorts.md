# Local Islands vs Private Resorts

## Summary
The Maldives offers two primary accommodation models: exclusive private island resorts and inhabited local islands with guesthouses. Local islands provide a more immersive cultural experience and substantial cost savings while maintaining modern amenities and tourist-friendly facilities.

## Key Facts
- Local-island guesthouses cost around $60/night versus significantly higher private resort prices
- Local islands have modest dress codes in town areas and alcohol restrictions
- Designated bikini beaches exist on local islands for tourist use while respecting local customs
- Local islands offer authentic dining at cafes and traditional Hedhikaa establishments at budget prices
- Private resorts remain available as lower-cost tier options (Embudu Village, Sun Island, Reethi Beach) for travelers wanting overwater experiences
