# Local Islands vs Resort Islands

## Summary
The Maldives distinguishes between resort islands, where international standards apply, and local islands, which observe stricter Islamic customs regarding dress and alcohol.

## Key Facts
- Alcohol is available on resort islands and liveaboards but not on local islands
- Resort islands permit standard holiday clothing
- Local islands expect modest dress with bikinis restricted to designated beaches
- Different cultural expectations and regulations apply to each island type
