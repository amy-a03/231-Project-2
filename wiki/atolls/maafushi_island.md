# Maafushi Island

## Summary
Maafushi is a local island in the Maldives offering budget-friendly guesthouses and mid-range hotels, making it an accessible base for travelers seeking alternatives to luxury resort islands.

## Key Facts
- Home to multiple budget and mid-range accommodation options including Kuredhi Beach Inn, Arena Beach Hotel, and Narnia Maldives Hotel
- Serves as an affordable alternative to private resort islands
- Easily accessible by speedboat from the main airport
- Offers direct beach access and local island experience
- Highlights in Booking.com's popular island destinations
- Home to Arena Beach Hotel at Maafushi
- Accessible and popular island for tourists
- Budget and mid-range accommodation availability
- Features a designated bikini beach
- Offers guesthouse accommodation
- Provides beach access with relaxed dress standards
- Maafushi is described as a popular base for budget stays
- The island provides convenient access for day trips
- It offers guesthouse-style accommodation rather than resort experiences
- Located within reasonable ferry distance from Malé
