# Malé City

## Summary
Malé, the capital of the Maldives, serves as the main entry point and transportation hub for travelers. It offers budget accommodation options and practical overnight stops when flight schedules require flexibility.

## Key Facts
- Malé is the primary airport entry point for most international travelers
- The city offers budget guesthouse accommodation
- Suitable as an overnight stop for inconvenient flight timings
- Ferry hub connecting to local islands and atolls
- Main transportation center for domestic flights and speedboat transfers
