# Marine Discovery Centres

## Summary
Educational and conservation hubs operated by Four Seasons resorts that facilitate coral restoration, turtle rehabilitation, marine education, and citizen science activities.

## Key Facts
- Serve as centers for coral frame building and propagation
- Conduct turtle release events as public education activities
- Support marine education programs for guests and local communities
- Enable citizen science initiatives and reef monitoring
- Located at Four Seasons Landaa Giraavaru and Kuda Huraa properties
