# Marine Heatwaves in the Maldives

## Summary
The intensification of marine heatwaves due to climate change poses a critical threat to coral ecosystems and marine biodiversity in the Maldives. These thermal stress events are measured through degree-heating days and drive coral bleaching and ecosystem degradation.

## Key Facts
- Marine heatwaves are intensifying in Maldivian waters
- Degree-heating days are a key metric for measuring coral thermal stress
- Heatwaves drive increases in coral bleaching and mortality
- Multiple climate scenarios project continued intensification
- Monitoring systems are needed to track heatwave frequency and intensity
