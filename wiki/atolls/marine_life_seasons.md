# Marine Life Seasons

## Summary
Seasonal patterns for observing specific marine species and wildlife in the Maldives, particularly whale sharks, manta rays, and sea turtles.

## Key Facts
- Whale sharks and manta rays are most commonly spotted between August and November
- Turtle nests hatch during the wet season (May to October), offering viewing opportunities
- Marine life abundance varies with seasonal currents and water conditions
- Warm ocean temperatures year-round support consistent marine ecosystem activity
