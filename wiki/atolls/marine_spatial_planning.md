# Marine Spatial Planning

## Summary
Strategic mapping and zoning of marine areas in Maldives to optimize ecosystem conservation, fisheries management, and climate adaptation. Marine spatial mapping enables identification of priority areas for reef restoration and habitat protection.

## Key Facts
- Marine spatial mapping is recommended for adaptation planning
- Spatial planning integrates fisheries management with ecosystem conservation
- Maps identify priority areas for coral reef restoration
- Zoning balances tourism, fisheries, and conservation objectives
- Data-driven approach improves allocation of adaptation resources
