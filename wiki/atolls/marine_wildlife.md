# Marine Wildlife

## Summary
The Maldives supports diverse marine life including manta rays, whale sharks, and sea turtles, with seasonal variations in visibility and species presence depending on plankton levels and water conditions.

## Key Facts
- Manta rays and whale sharks are attracted by increased plankton during rainy season (September-October)
- Sea turtles nest on beaches May-June with hatchlings in July-August
- Plankton levels correlate with season and affect both visibility and marine life presence
- Marine encounters are more memorable during plankton-rich rainy season despite lower visibility
