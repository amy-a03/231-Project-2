# Marine Wildlife Viewing

## Summary
The Maldives offers year-round opportunities to view marine life, including whale sharks, manta rays, and bioluminescent plankton, with different species and viewing conditions varying by season.

## Key Facts
- Whale sharks can be encountered in February and August
- Manta rays are commonly spotted in August and September
- Bioluminescent plankton can be observed on Vaadhoo Island in June
- August and September are recommended for swimming with manta rays and whale sharks
- Water visibility is reduced during monsoon months but marine life spotting remains good
