# North Malé Atoll

## Summary
Primary location of FAO reef fish resource survey, important fishing and tourism area in the Maldives with significant reef fish populations.

## Key Facts
- Subject of exploratory fishing trials by FAO
- Contains reef fish resources studied for abundance and exploitation
- Area of both local fishing activity and tourism development
- Fishing pressure documented through catch composition studies
