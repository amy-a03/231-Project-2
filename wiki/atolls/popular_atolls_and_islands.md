# Popular Atolls and Islands

## Summary
Different atolls within the Maldives experience varying weather patterns and are recommended for different seasons, with northern and southern atolls offering distinct advantages.

## Key Facts
- Southern atolls may see fewer rainy days in June compared to northern atolls, making them better for budget travelers
- Northern atolls are recommended over southern atolls in November for beach stays with fewer rainy days
- Vaadhoo Island is notable for bioluminescent plankton viewing in June
- Banana Reef is recommended for diving in January
- Male features the fish market and cultural sites including Mulee'aage Palace and the National Museum
