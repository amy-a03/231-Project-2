# Safer Island Strategy

## Summary
The Safer Island Strategy is a national adaptation initiative aimed at identifying and protecting strategically important islands from climate risks through infrastructure investment and resilience building.

## Key Facts
- Strategy focuses on designated 'safer islands' for concentrated development and protection
- Aims to reduce climate risk exposure for critical population and economic centers
- Involves coastal protection infrastructure and resilient development planning
- Complementary to broader NAPA adaptation framework
- Reflects triage approach to adaptation in resource-constrained setting
