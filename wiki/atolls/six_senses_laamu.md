# Six Senses Laamu

## Summary
A luxury resort in Laamu Atoll, Maldives, positioned as a leader in sustainable and responsible tourism. The property operates within a designated Hope Spot and Marine Protected Areas, integrating marine research, conservation, and community engagement into its core operations.

## Key Facts
- Located in the only atoll-designated Hope Spot in the Maldives
- Surrounded by Marine Protected Areas
- Operates SHELL (Sea Hub of Environmental Learning in Laamu), a marine science research and education center
- 2024 achievements include 1,350 hours of scientific surveys and 14,061 megafauna sightings
- Released 2,862,000 coral larvae onto house reef and protected 335,024 square meters of seagrass meadows
- Sourced 13,221 kilograms of sustainable local seafood in 2024
- Engaged 1,839 community members in conservation initiatives
