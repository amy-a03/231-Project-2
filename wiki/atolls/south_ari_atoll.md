# South Ari Atoll

## Summary
South Ari Atoll hosts luxury resort properties offering premium all-inclusive experiences with high-end amenities and pristine marine environments.

## Key Facts
- LUX* South Ari Atoll is a prominent luxury property in this region
- Known for high-end resort infrastructure and premium service standards
- Accessible by seaplane or speedboat transfer
- One of the few whale shark aggregation sites with year-round encounters
- Has one of the highest whale shark re-sighting rates in the world
- Primary research location for MWSRP
- Supports sustainable whale shark tourism and citizen science initiatives
