# South Ari Marine Protected Area

## Summary
The Maldives' largest marine protected area, established with baseline data support from the Maldives Whale Shark Research Programme. The protected area helps safeguard whale shark populations and their habitat.

## Key Facts
- Designated as the Maldives' largest marine protected area
- Creation was supported by baseline data from whale shark research programme
- Protects whale shark habitat and populations
- Represents commitment to long-term marine conservation in the Maldives
