# Ukulhas Island

## Summary
Ukulhas is a local island in the Maldives known for its strong house reef and excellent snorkeling opportunities, making it a popular guesthouse destination.

## Key Facts
- Features a strong house reef suitable for snorkeling
- Offers guesthouse accommodation for budget travelers
- Accessible for direct snorkeling from the island
