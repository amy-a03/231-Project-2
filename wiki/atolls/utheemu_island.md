# Utheemu Island

## Summary
Utheemu is a local island guesthouse destination characterized as a quieter alternative to more touristy island communities in the Maldives.

## Key Facts
- Described as a quieter local-island experience
- Offers guesthouse accommodation
- Less touristy than some other guesthouse destinations
