# Vaadhoo Island

## Summary
Vaadhoo Island is the most famous location in the Maldives for viewing the bioluminescent 'Sea of Stars' phenomenon. The island's shores glow with plankton activity, particularly during the June–December window on new moon nights.

## Key Facts
- Famous for the bioluminescent 'Sea of Stars' effect along its shoreline
- Best viewing period: June through December
- Optimal conditions: new moon nights with maximum darkness
- Nighttime plankton glow creates a distinctive blue luminescence in the water
