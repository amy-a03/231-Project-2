# Water Visibility and Marine Life

## Summary
Water visibility and marine wildlife encounters are directly influenced by seasonal conditions, with high visibility during dry season and unique wildlife viewing opportunities during monsoon months despite lower visibility.

## Key Facts
- February offers ideal water conditions for snorkeling and underwater activities
- May water visibility is lower due to rainfall and plankton
- Manta rays and whale sharks are commonly spotted in August and September despite reduced visibility
- Bioluminescent plankton can be seen on Vaadhoo Island in June
- October is the last good month for surfing as southwest monsoon begins to ease
- August and September are good times to swim with manta rays and whale sharks
