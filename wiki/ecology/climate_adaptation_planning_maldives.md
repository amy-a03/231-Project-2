# Climate Adaptation Planning Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives has developed national adaptation frameworks including the NAPA and Safer Island Strategy to address climate risks through resilient infrastructure, coastal protection, and water security measures.

## Key Facts
- The Maldives NAPA (National Adaptation Programme of Action) guides climate adaptation policy
- Safer Island Strategy focuses on protecting inhabited and important islands
- Adaptation priorities include climate-resilient infrastructure development
- Coastal-zone risk reduction is a key adaptation pillar
- Water-security measures address freshwater lens protection and saltwater intrusion
