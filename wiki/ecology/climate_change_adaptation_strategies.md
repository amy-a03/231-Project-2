# Climate Change Adaptation Strategies

## Summary
Approaches for the Maldives to adapt to increasing coastal flooding and sea-level rise. Adaptation must be tailored to individual islands based on their specific vulnerability profiles and flood-risk assessments.

## Key Facts
- Adaptation needs must be ranked and prioritized by individual island
- Methods can determine how much elevation increase is needed per island
- Authorities can use flood models to compare different protection strategies
- Rapid assessment of wave-induced flooding under changing scenarios enables strategic planning
