# Climate Change Impacts on Maldivian Reefs

## Summary
Global climate change poses a primary threat to Maldivian coral reefs through thermal stress and bleaching events. The study demonstrates how widespread thermal anomalies interact with and amplify local human impacts.

## Key Facts
- Thermal anomalies are a primary driver of reef community change
- Global climate stress operates alongside localized anthropogenic pressures
- Repeated bleaching events alter long-term reef trajectory
- Climate impacts affect multiple trophic levels in reef ecosystems
