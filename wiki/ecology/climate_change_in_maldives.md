# Climate Change in Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives faces existential climate threats due to being the world's lowest-lying islands. Climate risk is a defining characteristic of the destination and shapes discussions around tourism and sustainability.

## Key Facts
- The Maldives are the world's lowest-lying islands
- Face significant climate and sea level rise threats
- Climate risk is a central narrative in tourism and travel discourse
- Environmental significance tied to climate vulnerability
