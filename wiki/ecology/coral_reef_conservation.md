# Coral Reef Conservation

## Summary
Coral reef biodiversity protection is identified as a core climate adaptation priority for the Maldives, recognizing the critical ecological and economic role of reefs in supporting fisheries, tourism, and coastal protection.

## Key Facts
- Coral reef biodiversity is designated as a key adaptation priority
- Reefs provide critical coastal protection against erosion and storm surge
- Reef health directly supports fisheries sector livelihoods
- Tourism economy is dependent on healthy coral ecosystems
- Maldivian coral reefs have exceptional biodiversity
- Reefs provide critical protective functions against storms and erosion
- Reefs support vital fisheries and livelihoods
- Reefs have major economic value to the tourism industry
- Reefs are threatened by rising sea temperatures, ocean acidification, and bleaching
