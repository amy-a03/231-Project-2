# Coral Reef Ecosystems

## Summary
Coral reefs are the most biodiverse and ecologically important habitat within Baa Atoll's biosphere reserve. They support exceptional marine species diversity including corals, fish, rays, sharks, and turtles.

## Key Facts
- Approximately 250 coral species present in Baa Atoll
- Support 1,200 reef and reef-associated fish species
- Identified as the most important habitat for both area and biodiversity in the reserve
- Host manta rays, whale sharks, and marine turtles
- Lagoon and channel habitats support seagrass beds and mangroves as complementary ecosystems
