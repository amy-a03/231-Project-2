# Coral Reef Resilience Strategy

## Summary
The World Bank's roadmap for adapting Maldives' coral reef ecosystems to climate change impacts, focusing on reef restoration, monitoring, and nature-based solutions. Addresses threats from marine heatwaves and changing ocean conditions through ecosystem conservation and community engagement.

## Key Facts
- Climate change will intensify marine heatwaves in the Maldives
- Degree-heating days for coral areas are projected to increase significantly
- Recommended interventions include coral reef restoration and mangrove protection
- Marine spatial mapping is essential for adaptation planning
- Community participation in habitat monitoring strengthens resilience
- Nature-based solutions combining reefs and mangroves are prioritized
