# Fisheries Management and Climate Change

## Summary
Analysis of how climate change threatens Maldives' fisheries sector through reduced fish catches and habitat shifts, with recommendations for economic diversification and alternative livelihoods. Critical for food security and economic stability in communities outside tourism zones.

## Key Facts
- Fisheries support food security, livelihoods, and local economies in Maldives
- Fish catches in the EEZ are projected to decline under multiple climate scenarios
- Species habitat suitability is shifting across the archipelago
- Alternatives to fisheries-dependent livelihoods must be explored
- Research and capacity building in fisheries management are essential
- Socioeconomic resilience of coastal communities must be increased
