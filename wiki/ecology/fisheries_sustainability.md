# Fisheries Sustainability

## Summary
Fisheries are identified as a critical adaptation priority sector in the Maldives, vulnerable to both climate change impacts and resource sustainability challenges that require resilience-building measures.

## Key Facts
- Fisheries sector is designated as a core adaptation priority
- Sector depends on healthy coral reef ecosystems
- Climate adaptation measures aim to increase sector resilience
- Food security concerns linked to fisheries sustainability
