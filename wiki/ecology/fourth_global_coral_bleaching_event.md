# Fourth Global Coral Bleaching Event

## Summary
NOAA confirmed in 2024 that the world is experiencing the fourth recorded global coral bleaching event, with the second occurrence in the last 10 years. The event is characterized by bleaching-level heat stress across the Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Ocean basins, including the Maldives region.

## Key Facts
- Fourth global coral bleaching event on record confirmed by NOAA
- Second global bleaching event to occur within the last 10 years
- Heat stress detected across Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Ocean basins
- Tracked using satellite-based Coral Reef Watch heat-stress monitoring
- Sea-surface-temperature data analyzed from 1985 to present
- Indian Ocean basin affected, directly impacting Maldives reef conditions
