# Indian Ocean Coral Ecosystems

## Summary
The Indian Ocean region, including the Maldives, was significantly affected by coral bleaching heat stress during the 2014-2017 global bleaching event. The region is monitored by NOAA Coral Reef Watch for ongoing heat stress impacts.

## Key Facts
- Indian Ocean experienced bleaching-level heat stress during 2014-2017 event
- Maldives is part of Indian Ocean coral reef monitoring networks
- Southeast Asian coral reefs in the Indian Ocean basin were severely impacted
- Region requires continued satellite-based monitoring for heat stress
