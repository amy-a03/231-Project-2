# Local Human Impacts on Maldivian Reefs

## Summary
Tourism development and coastal modification activities including dredging create localized pressures that compound the effects of global climate stress on Maldivian coral reef communities.

## Key Facts
- Tourism-related activities contribute to reef degradation
- Dredging operations directly damage reef structure
- Local pressures interact with and amplify global climate impacts
- Human impacts create stress that reduces reef resilience to bleaching
