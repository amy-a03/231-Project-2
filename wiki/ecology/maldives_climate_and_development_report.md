# Maldives Climate and Development Report

## Summary
A comprehensive World Bank country assessment that integrates climate risks, development priorities, and adaptation strategies for the Maldives. The report uses quantitative risk assessments including sea level rise modeling to inform policy recommendations.

## Key Facts
- Supports integrated climate and development planning for Maldives
- Incorporates quantitative risk assessment of sea-level-rise-induced coastal flooding
- Identifies adaptation as economically beneficial even at mild levels of implementation
- Highlights need for more detailed island-level flood protection information
