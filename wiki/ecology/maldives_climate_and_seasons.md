# Maldives Climate and Seasons

## Summary
The Maldives has a warm, humid tropical climate with stable year-round temperatures between 26–31°C. The year divides into a dry season (December–April), monsoon season (May–November), and shoulder seasons (April, November), each with distinct weather patterns and travel characteristics.

## Key Facts
- Daytime temperatures range from 26°C to 31°C year-round, with sea temperatures around 28°C
- Southwest monsoon occurs early May to late August; Northeast monsoon occurs late September through December
- Monsoon showers are typically brief with quick returns to sunshine
- Dry season (December–April) offers low humidity, sunny skies, and calm seas
- January–March provide the strongest underwater visibility for snorkeling and diving
