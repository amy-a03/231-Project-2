# Maldives Climate Overview

## Summary
General climate characteristics of the Maldives, featuring consistently warm tropical weather with seasonal variation in rainfall and wind patterns.

## Key Facts
- More than 1,000 islands with approximately 200 inhabited islands
- Consistently warm weather year-round with temperatures ranging from 77°F to 90°F depending on season
- Average dry season temperatures around 82°F with 8+ hours of daily sunshine
- Tropical monsoon system creates distinct wet (May-October) and dry (November-April) seasons
- No truly bad time to visit, with each season offering distinct advantages for different traveler priorities
