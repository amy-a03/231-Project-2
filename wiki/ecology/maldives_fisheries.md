# Maldives Fisheries

## Summary
Economic sector combining traditional reef fish harvesting with modern export activities and tourism-related fishing pressure in the Maldives.

## Key Facts
- Reef fish represent local consumption and export commodities
- Subject to various fishing gear methods and intensities
- Resources studied for sustainability and development potential
- Faces competing pressures from tourism development and local economic needs
