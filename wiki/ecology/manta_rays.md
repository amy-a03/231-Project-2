# Manta Rays

## Summary
Manta rays are attracted to the Maldives during the rainy season when plankton levels increase, particularly in September and October, offering divers opportunities for memorable marine encounters.

## Key Facts
- Manta rays appear during rainy season when plankton increases
- September and October are particularly good for manta ray sightings
- Plankton-rich waters attract manta rays despite reducing visibility
