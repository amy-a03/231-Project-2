# Manta Trust

## Summary
Manta Trust is a global conservation organization dedicated to protecting manta rays and devil rays through research, education, and policy collaboration. The organization has a long-standing presence in the Maldives, contributing to both manta research and sustainable tourism guidance.

## Key Facts
- Works across 10 species of manta and devil rays worldwide
- Primary threat to manta rays is commercial fisheries
- Operates through global affiliate networks for collaborative conservation
- Engages in research, education, policy support, and conservation collaboration
- Has established presence in Maldivian manta research and tourism guidance
- Vision is a sustainable ocean future where manta rays thrive in healthy marine ecosystems
