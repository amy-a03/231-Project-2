# Manta Trust Research

## Summary
The Manta Trust conducts scientific research on reef manta ray ecology and conservation in the Maldives. Their studies, such as Armstrong et al. 2021, provide evidence-based insights for marine protection and sustainable tourism management.

## Key Facts
- Operates a research portal documenting manta ray studies in the Maldives
- Armstrong et al. 2021 study examined feeding behavior in Hanifaru Bay
- Research links manta behavior with environmental and zooplankton conditions
- Findings support evidence-based marine protected area management
- Research contributes to sustainable tourism practices in the Maldives
