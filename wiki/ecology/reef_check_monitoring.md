# Reef Check Monitoring

## Summary
A standardized survey methodology used in the Maldives study to assess coral reef health by measuring benthic composition, fish abundance, and macro-invertebrate populations over time.

## Key Facts
- Standardized reef assessment methodology applied across four Maldivian sites
- Measures benthic (sea floor) composition
- Tracks fish abundance as indicator of reef health
- Monitors macro-invertebrate populations
- Enables long-term comparative analysis of reef condition changes
