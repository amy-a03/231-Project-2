# Reef Ecosystem Health

## Summary
The overall condition of coral reef ecosystems in atoll environments, measured through fish population surveys and catch composition analysis. Health monitoring is essential in areas experiencing tourism development pressure.

## Key Facts
- Fish catch composition is a key indicator of reef ecosystem condition
- Baseline data collection enables detection of ecosystem changes
- Atoll reef systems require ongoing monitoring due to human pressures
- Sustainable yield levels serve as thresholds for ecosystem health
