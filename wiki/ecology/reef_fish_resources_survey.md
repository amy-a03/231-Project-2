# Reef Fish Resources Survey

## Summary
FAO exploratory fishing trials conducted in North Malé Atoll to assess reef fish resource status, fishing gear efficiency, and potential for sustainable reef fish fishery development in the Maldives.

## Key Facts
- Study focused on North Malé Atoll reef fish resources and exploitation rates
- Evaluated efficiency of different fishing gears used in reef fish harvesting
- Reef fish were already part of local consumption and export activities
- Survey assessed catch composition and fishing intensity patterns
- Predates modern tourism boom but relevant to understanding resource pressures
- Aimed to determine abundance and viability of reef fish as a commercial resource
