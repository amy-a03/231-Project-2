# Reef Monitoring Systems

## Summary
Framework for systematic monitoring of coral reef health, species composition, and thermal stress in the Maldives. Community-based monitoring programs enhance local engagement and data collection capacity for adaptive management.

## Key Facts
- Better reef-fish monitoring is essential for adaptation planning
- Community participation in habitat monitoring is recommended
- Monitoring systems track coral bleaching and thermal stress events
- Data informs adaptive management decisions
- Local capacity building strengthens monitoring effectiveness
