# Reef Recovery and Resilience

## Summary
Coral reef communities demonstrate variable capacity for recovery after bleaching and disturbance events. Understanding recovery patterns helps identify which reef areas and management strategies are most effective for conservation.

## Key Facts
- Recovery trajectories differ by location and reef characteristics
- Resilience is reduced by cumulative local human impacts
- Eleven-year monitoring reveals recovery rates and timing
- Resilience depends on interaction between global and local stressors
