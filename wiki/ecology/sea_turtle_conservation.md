# Sea Turtle Conservation

## Summary
Sea turtles nest on Maldivian beaches during May and June, with hatchlings emerging in July and August. Conservation efforts focus on protecting nesting sites from disruptive light and noise.

## Key Facts
- Sea turtles begin nesting in May and continue through June
- Turtle hatchlings emerge around July and August
- Conservation projects protect nesting areas from disruptive light pollution and noise
- Nesting period aligns with the beginning of the rainy season
