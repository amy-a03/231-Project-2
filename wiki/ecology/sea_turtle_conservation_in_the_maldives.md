# Sea Turtle Conservation in the Maldives

## Summary
Rescue and rehabilitation programs at Four Seasons resorts have become a significant component of marine species protection in the Maldives.

## Key Facts
- More than 280 sea turtles rescued and rehabilitated since program inception
- Turtle releases conducted at Marine Discovery Centres as public education events
- Integrated with broader marine conservation strategy of Four Seasons resorts
- Contributes to citizen science initiatives in partnership with local communities
- Program supports species monitoring and ecosystem health assessment
