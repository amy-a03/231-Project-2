# Sustainable Fisheries Management

## Summary
The practice of monitoring and maintaining fish catch levels below unsustainable thresholds to preserve reef ecosystems. Central Maldives case study demonstrates importance of baseline data collection and ongoing monitoring.

## Key Facts
- Unsustainable fishery thresholds exist as published benchmarks for comparison
- Baseline surveys are critical for detecting changes in fishery status over time
- Tourism-driven demand can accelerate pressure on fish populations
- Continued monitoring systems are essential for early warning of sustainability issues
