# Sustainable Fishing in the Maldives

## Summary
Maldivian cuisine relies heavily on sustainably caught tuna, which is a cornerstone ingredient in traditional dishes. This sustainable fishing practice reflects the archipelago's commitment to preserving marine resources while maintaining culinary traditions.

## Key Facts
- Tuna is the primary protein source in Maldivian cuisine
- Fishing practices emphasize sustainability and responsible resource management
- Sustainably caught seafood is featured prominently in resort and guesthouse menus
- Local fishing traditions are integral to the islands' food culture and economy
