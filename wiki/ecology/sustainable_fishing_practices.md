# Sustainable Fishing Practices

## Summary
Sustainable fishing in the Maldives involves partnerships between resorts and local fishing communities to source seafood responsibly while maintaining livelihoods and reducing ecological impact.

## Key Facts
- Six Senses Laamu sourced 13,221 kilograms of sustainable local seafood in 2024
- Works directly with local fishers through sustainable seafood program
- Aims to support community livelihoods while reducing ecological impact
- Part of broader social responsibility and conservation strategy
- Balances tourism demand with environmental and community wellbeing
