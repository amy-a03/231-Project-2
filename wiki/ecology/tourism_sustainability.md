# Tourism Sustainability

## Summary
Tourism is a critical adaptation sector in the Maldives, with 45% of tourist resorts already experiencing severe beach erosion. Sustainable tourism development requires climate-resilient infrastructure and coastal protection strategies.

## Key Facts
- Tourism is a designated adaptation priority sector
- 45% of tourist resorts report severe beach erosion
- Coastal zone adaptation directly supports tourism resilience
- Infrastructure resilience is essential for maintaining tourism economy
