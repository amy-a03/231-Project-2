# Whale Shark and Manta Ray Season

## Summary
October and November are the prime months for encountering whale sharks and manta rays in the Maldives, as plankton blooms during this period attract these filter-feeding species. This seasonal window combines good weather with exceptional wildlife viewing opportunities.

## Key Facts
- Peak season for whale sharks and manta rays: October and November
- Marine megafauna are attracted by increased plankton concentrations
- These months offer warm temperatures despite being in the transitional/wet season
- Travel offers are commonly available during October-November, providing good value
- Plankton blooms occur from May through December but peak in October-November
- Peak season for whale sharks and manta rays runs May through November
- Baa, Ari, and North Mal atolls are primary regions for these encounters during peak season
- Plankton blooms attract whale sharks and manta rays during the May-November window
- South Ari Marine Park sustains consistent sightings year-round
