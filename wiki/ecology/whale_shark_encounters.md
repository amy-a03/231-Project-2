# Whale Shark Encounters

## Summary
Whale sharks are a signature megafauna species in the Maldives, most commonly sighted during August–November when monsoon currents bring nutrient-rich waters that support their plankton-feeding behavior.

## Key Facts
- Whale sharks are most frequently encountered August–November during the monsoon season
- They are attracted by nutrient-rich currents that support plankton blooms along atoll walls and channels
- Giant turtles are also common sightings during the same nutrient-rich season
- Whale shark sightings are among the key attractions marketed by dive resorts and liveaboards
