# Accommodation and Lodging Options

## Summary
The Maldives offers various lodging options from luxury private-island resorts to locally-run guesthouses and vacation rentals. Budget-conscious travelers can find significant savings during low season through discounted overwater bungalows and alternative accommodations.

## Key Facts
- Overwater bungalows may have meaningful discounts during May to October low season
- Locally run guesthouses and vacation rentals are cheaper than high-end private-island resorts
- Popular resorts and single-resort islands have varying crowd levels
- Package deals are more common during rainy season (May to October)
