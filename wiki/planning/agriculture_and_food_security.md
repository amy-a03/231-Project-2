# Agriculture and Food Security

## Summary
Agriculture and food security are identified as adaptation priorities in the Maldives, addressing limited arable land, climate impacts on crop production, and the need for sustainable food systems.

## Key Facts
- Agriculture and food security is a designated adaptation priority
- Limited arable land constrains agricultural production
- Climate adaptation measures support food security resilience
- Sector closely linked to water resources management
