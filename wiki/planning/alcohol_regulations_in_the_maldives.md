# Alcohol Regulations in the Maldives

## Summary
Alcohol consumption in the Maldives is permitted on resort islands but restricted on local islands, reflecting the country's Muslim culture and legal framework.

## Key Facts
- Alcohol is prohibited on local islands
- Alcohol is permitted on resort islands
- Alcohol cannot be imported into the country
- Alcohol rules differ significantly between resort and local island environments
- Understanding alcohol restrictions is essential for travel planning
