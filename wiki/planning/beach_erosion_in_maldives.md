# Beach Erosion in Maldives

## Summary
Beach erosion is a critical climate-related threat affecting both inhabited islands and tourist infrastructure in the Maldives, with over 60% of inhabited islands experiencing severe erosion.

## Key Facts
- 62% of inhabited islands report severe beach erosion
- 45% of tourist resorts have reported severe beach erosion
- Erosion is linked to sea-level rise, storm surge, and coastal hydrodynamics
- Beach loss threatens both residential settlements and tourism-dependent economies
- Erosion compounds saltwater intrusion into freshwater resources
