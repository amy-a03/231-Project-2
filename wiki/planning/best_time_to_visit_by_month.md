# Best Time to Visit by Month

## Summary
A comprehensive month-by-month guide to visiting the Maldives, detailing weather conditions, water activities, pricing, and recommended experiences for each month of the year.

## Key Facts
- January offers blue skies, warm days, and excellent diving conditions with short afternoon rain spells possible
- March is the hottest month (around 84°F) and remains high season, requiring advance booking
- April marks the end of high season with increased rain risk but better travel deals
- May begins Ramadan with more rainfall, higher humidity, and unpredictable weather including possible storms
- June through October sees the southwest monsoon with cloudy days, frequent rain, and lower water visibility
- August is hot and humid with heavy but short downpours; resorts offer significant discounts
- November sees tourism return with brighter days and occasional afternoon showers
- July offers good surfing conditions with average swells of 2 to 8 feet
