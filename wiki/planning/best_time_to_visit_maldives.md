# Best Time to Visit Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives offers pleasant weather year-round, with distinct dry and wet seasons. The ideal visit window is November through April when conditions are warm and dry, though May through November offers better value with shorter rain showers.

## Key Facts
- Dry season (November–April): warm, average 86°F / 30°C, little rain, low humidity
- Peak season (January–April): driest months with clear skies, full activity availability, and highest prices
- Monsoon season (June–August): wetter with sudden downpours, though often short-lived
- Best value period (May–November): resorts offer lower rates, free transfers, upgrades, and reduced prices
- May and September–October are recommended as middle-ground months with less rain than June–August
- Recommended trip length: 7–10 days to experience variety including island hopping
- Year-round packing essentials: swimwear, SPF 30+ sunscreen, sarong for beach versatility
