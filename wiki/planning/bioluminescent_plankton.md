# Bioluminescent Plankton

## Summary
Bioluminescent plankton creates stunning nighttime light displays on Maldivian beaches, appearing more frequently during the rainy season due to higher plankton abundance. This natural phenomenon attracts visitors seeking unique evening experiences.

## Key Facts
- Bioluminescent plankton is more common during rainy season due to increased plankton abundance
- The phenomenon lights up beaches at night with glowing displays
