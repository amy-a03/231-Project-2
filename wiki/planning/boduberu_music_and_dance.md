# Boduberu Music and Dance

## Summary
Boduberu is a traditional Maldivian musical and dance form that accompanies social gatherings and hospitality experiences. It represents an important element of cultural immersion for visitors experiencing authentic island life.

## Key Facts
- Boduberu music is performed during social gatherings and hospitality events
- It is a distinctive element of Maldivian cultural experiences on local islands
- Boduberu performances often accompany shared meals and community gatherings
- The tradition is particularly visible in local island homestay and guesthouse experiences
