# Budget Accommodation in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives offers affordable lodging options beyond luxury resorts, primarily through local-island guesthouses that cost around $60–$120 per night. Staying on inhabited islands provides modern comfort while significantly reducing travel costs compared to private resort stays.

## Key Facts
- Local-island guesthouses average $60–$120 per night, compared to much higher private resort prices
- Kaani Village (Maafushi) costs around $70/night; Season Paradise (Thulusdhoo) around $90/night; Boutique Beach (Dhigurah) around $120/night
- Budget resorts like Embudu Village, Sun Island, and Reethi Beach offer overwater lodging at lower-cost tier pricing
- Local islands require modest dress expectations in town and have alcohol restrictions, with designated bikini beaches for tourists
- Guesthouses provide modern amenities including air conditioning despite modest pricing
- Local island stays (Maafushi, Dhiffushi) are the most budget-friendly accommodation option
- Mid-range resorts like Summer Island and Paradise Island offer more affordable alternatives to luxury properties
- Malé serves as a practical overnight stop for travelers with inconvenient flight timings
- Local islands require modest dress, no public swimwear outside designated beaches, and alcohol is not sold
- Budget accommodation accessibility varies by island location and distance from the airport
