# Budget Travel Planning

## Summary
Strategies for visiting the Maldives at lower costs, primarily during low season (May to November) with various cost-saving tactics.

## Key Facts
- Low season (May to November) offers the cheapest rates with hotels reducing prices substantially
- Common hotel promotions include free transfers, free nights, and room upgrades
- Choosing speedboat transfers instead of seaplanes saves over $250 per trip
- New hotel openings often feature introductory deals in their first months
- Low-season temperatures remain in the mid-80s°F despite higher rainfall risk
