# Budget Travel Planning for the Maldives

## Summary
A seven-day budget visit to the Maldives can be achieved for approximately $970 by combining local-island stays, local dining, and strategic transport choices. This planning approach breaks down costs across accommodation, food, transfers, and miscellaneous expenses.

## Key Facts
- Sample seven-day budget: $500 accommodation, $350 food/dining, $80 transfers, $40 miscellaneous = $970 total
- Speedboat transfers should be used strategically instead of ferries when timing justifies the cost difference
- Local cafes and Hedhikaa (traditional food establishments) provide affordable dining alternatives to resort restaurants
- Budget-conscious travelers can experience overwater bungalows through split stays and entry-level luxury resorts
- Transfer costs average approximately $80 for a week-long visit using cost-effective options
