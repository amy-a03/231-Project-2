# Budget Travel to the Maldives

## Summary
Budget-conscious travelers can maximize value by visiting during monsoon season (May–November) when prices drop and resorts are quieter. Shoulder seasons offer additional balance between cost savings and acceptable weather.

## Key Facts
- May–November (monsoon season) offers lower prices and quieter resorts
- Budget travelers save significantly compared to peak season rates
- Shoulder seasons (April, November) provide moderate pricing and fewer crowds
- Brief monsoon showers do not significantly impact the travel experience
