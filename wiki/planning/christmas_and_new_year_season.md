# Christmas and New Year Season

## Summary
Peak tourist season during winter holidays when weather is consistently sunny and warm. Major resorts offer special festive experiences and celebrations.

## Key Facts
- Part of the broader November to April dry season
- Features sunny weather with temperatures between 77°F and 90°F
- High visitor numbers and peak hotel rates
- Resorts offer special events: dinners, entertainment, Christmas tree lighting, Santa visits, beach movie nights, and New Year's celebrations
- Noted resorts include Six Senses, Gili Lankanfushi, The Nautilus, and Joali
