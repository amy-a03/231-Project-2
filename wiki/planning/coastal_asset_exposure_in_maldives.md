# Coastal Asset Exposure in Maldives

## Summary
Analysis of how Maldives' economic assets are distributed relative to coastal hazards. The assessment reveals significant concentrations of buildings and infrastructure within high-risk zones near shorelines across the archipelago.

## Key Facts
- Total capital stock modeled at approximately US$24.5 billion
- 36% of buildings located within 100 m of shoreline
- 71% of buildings within 200 m of shoreline
- Economic assets heavily concentrated in coastal zones increases vulnerability
- Airports benefit from elevated construction providing relative protection
