# Coastal Community Resilience

## Summary
Framework for strengthening the adaptive capacity of Maldivian coastal communities to climate change through livelihood diversification, socioeconomic support, and enhanced participation in environmental management. Addresses vulnerability in fishing-dependent areas.

## Key Facts
- Coastal communities in Maldives have high dependence on fisheries and marine resources
- Socioeconomic resilience must be increased through livelihood diversification
- Community participation in habitat monitoring enhances resilience outcomes
- Training and capacity building are critical for adaptation
- Alternative income sources beyond fisheries are necessary
- Local knowledge integration improves adaptation effectiveness
