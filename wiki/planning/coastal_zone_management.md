# Coastal Zone Management

## Summary
Coastal zone management is a primary adaptation priority for the Maldives, addressing severe beach erosion affecting both inhabited islands and tourist resorts through integrated protection and resilience strategies.

## Key Facts
- Coastal zones are a designated adaptation priority
- 62% of inhabited islands report severe beach erosion
- 45% of tourist resorts report severe beach erosion
- Adaptation measures include erosion control and coastal protection infrastructure
