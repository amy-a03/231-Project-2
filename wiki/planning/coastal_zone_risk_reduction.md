# Coastal Zone Risk Reduction

## Summary
Coastal zone management is a critical adaptation priority in the Maldives, addressing risks from sea-level rise, storm surge, erosion, and saltwater intrusion through integrated planning and protective infrastructure.

## Key Facts
- Coastal zones are the primary inhabited and economically productive areas
- Risk reduction requires integrated hazard mapping and early warning systems
- Protective infrastructure includes seawalls, breakwaters, and beach nourishment
- Land-use planning and building codes must account for climate projections
- Nature-based solutions like mangrove and coral restoration provide co-benefits
