# Culture Trip Maldives Hub

## Summary
Culture Trip's curated content hub for the Maldives covering accommodations, dining, activities, and sustainable travel guidance. The platform combines travel inspiration with bookable small-group guided itineraries led by local insiders.

## Key Facts
- Hub includes guides on luxury hotels, budget-friendly accommodations, and resort recommendations
- Content covers food and drink, things to do, places to stay, culture, and inspiration stories
- Small-group trips led by local guides combine expert curation with hands-on travel experiences
- Features sustainability-focused guides for greener Maldives travel
- Includes specialized content on activities like surfing alongside relaxation and cultural experiences
- Covers natural attractions such as the Sea of Stars bioluminescent phenomenon
