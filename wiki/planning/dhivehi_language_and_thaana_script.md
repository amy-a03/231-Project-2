# Dhivehi Language and Thaana Script

## Summary
Dhivehi is the national language of the Maldives, written in the unique Thaana script which developed from Arabic numerals around the 16th century. Understanding this language is fundamental to Maldivian identity and culture.

## Key Facts
- Dhivehi is the official national language of the Maldives
- Thaana is the unique script used to write Dhivehi
- Thaana script developed from Arabic numerals around the 16th century
- The script reflects the country's cultural connections across the Indian Ocean
