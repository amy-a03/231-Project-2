# Dhoni Ferry Service

## Summary
Public ferry dhonis provide a budget-friendly but slower transport option, running on a weekly schedule from Malé City to islands throughout Ari Atoll.

## Key Facts
- Public ferry dhonis operate from Malé City to Ari Atoll islands
- Weekly schedule available
- Budget-friendly transport option
- Slower transit times compared to speedboats and seaplanes
