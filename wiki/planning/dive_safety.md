# Dive Safety

## Summary
Dive safety on liveaboards requires proper training, equipment maintenance, and adherence to established protocols. Reputable operators maintain emergency systems and enforce strict diving procedures.

## Key Facts
- Emergency oxygen and first aid equipment must be available on all vessels
- Detailed dive planning required before each dive
- Divers must respect depth limits appropriate to their certification
- Buddy procedures and surface intervals are mandatory
- Medical evacuation coverage essential for remote locations like the Maldives
