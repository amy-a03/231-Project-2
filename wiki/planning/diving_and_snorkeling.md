# Diving and Snorkeling

## Summary
The Maldives is renowned for diving and snorkeling, with visibility and marine life varying seasonally. The dry season offers the best clarity, while the rainy season attracts larger marine species despite reduced visibility.

## Key Facts
- Underwater visibility is excellent during dry season (December-April)
- Visibility is reduced during rainy season (May-November) due to increased plankton
- Plankton increase during rainy season attracts manta rays and whale sharks
- January and February offer peak diving and snorkeling conditions
