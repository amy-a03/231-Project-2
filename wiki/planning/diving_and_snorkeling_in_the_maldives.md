# Diving and Snorkeling in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives offers world-class diving and snorkeling opportunities year-round, with peak underwater visibility from January to April. Manta ray encounters are particularly common during these months.

## Key Facts
- Underwater visibility is strongest from January to April
- Diving remains possible year-round in the Maldives
- January–March are highlighted for manta ray encounters
- Diving requires clear water and calm conditions, making dry season optimal
- Underwater visibility extends to 40 metres at popular dive sites
- Offers spectacular dive and snorkel sites distributed across multiple atolls
- Deep-sea diving is a major attraction for the destination
- Coral atoll structure supports diverse marine ecosystems ideal for diving exploration
