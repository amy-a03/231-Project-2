# Domestic Air Transport

## Summary
Domestic aviation network connecting Velana International Airport to airports across the atolls, enabling inter-atoll travel for tourists and residents.

## Key Facts
- Domestic airports located across multiple atolls
- Provides connectivity from main international airport to resort destinations
- Part of primary domestic movement options alongside ferries and speedboats
- Used for longer-distance atoll transfers
