# Dress Code in the Maldives

## Summary
Dress expectations in the Maldives vary significantly between resort islands and local islands. Resort islands permit casual beachwear and swimwear, while local islands require modest, respectful clothing that covers shoulders and knees in public spaces.

## Key Facts
- Resort islands allow casual beach clothing including swimwear, shorts, and tank tops
- Local islands require unrevealing and respectful public clothing with shoulders and knees covered
- Bikinis are limited to designated bikini beaches on local islands
- Restaurant dress codes at resorts recommend smart casual or casual wear rather than swimwear or gym clothing
- Traditional Maldivian clothing like Feyli and Libaas is welcomed and appreciated by locals
- Conservative dress is expected near mosques on local islands
- Travelers should pack lightweight layers and cover-ups when visiting both resort and local islands
