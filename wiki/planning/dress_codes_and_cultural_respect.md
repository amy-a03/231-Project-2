# Dress Codes and Cultural Respect

## Summary
The Maldives is a Muslim country where modest behavior and clothing are expected outside resort islands. Visitors should understand and respect local customs when visiting communities and public areas.

## Key Facts
- Maldives is a Muslim country requiring respectful behavior outside resort islands
- Modest clothing is expected when visiting local communities
- Lightweight, respectful clothing is appropriate for interacting with communities
- Swimwear rules differ between resort islands and local islands
- Bikini beaches on local islands are designated areas where swimwear is permitted
