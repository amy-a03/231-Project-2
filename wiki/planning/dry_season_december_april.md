# Dry Season (December-April)

## Summary
The dry season from December through April is the peak tourist season in the Maldives, characterized by sunny, warm weather with low humidity, excellent underwater visibility, and ideal conditions for diving and snorkeling.

## Key Facts
- December-April offers hot, dry, sunny weather with temperatures around 30-32°C
- February is the driest month of the year with minimal rainfall
- Underwater visibility is excellent throughout the dry season, ideal for diving and snorkeling
- January is peak season requiring advance booking due to high demand
- April marks the transition end with possible short showers and marks the beginning of surf season in southern atolls
