# Dry Season (November to April)

## Summary
The optimal period for visiting the Maldives, characterized by clear skies, minimal rainfall, and excellent water visibility. This season is ideal for beach activities, snorkeling, and diving.

## Key Facts
- Runs from November to April
- Features more than eight hours of sun per day with average temperatures around 82°F
- Provides strong snorkeling and diving visibility due to northeast-flowing currents
- Eastern atolls offer the best visibility during this season
- Clear skies and minimal rainfall make it ideal for water sports and beach time
