# EarthCheck Certification

## Summary
An environmental certification program that measures sustainability performance across multiple indicators. Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru has maintained continuous EarthCheck certification since 2012.

## Key Facts
- Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru received EarthCheck Gold Certification in 2022
- The resort has maintained continuous certification since 2012 with annual re-assessment
- Measured indicators include energy consumption, emissions, water use, waste generation, paper use, and chemical/pesticide use
- Community involvement is tracked as part of certification requirements
- Gold Certification represents the highest achievement level in the EarthCheck program
- Four Seasons Maldives resorts hold EarthCheck Silver certification
- Working toward EarthCheck Gold certification
- Provides third-party validation of sustainability practices
- Used as benchmark for operational metrics and environmental performance
- Supports transparent reporting of conservation and community impact
