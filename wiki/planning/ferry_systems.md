# Ferry Systems

## Summary
Scheduled ferry service connecting Malé to atolls throughout the Maldives, providing affordable public transport for inter-island movement.

## Key Facts
- Scheduled ferries operate from Malé to many atolls
- Primary domestic movement option alongside domestic flights
- Provides public transport alternative to resort-arranged transfers
- Serves both tourists and resident populations
