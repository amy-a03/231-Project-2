# Green Globe Certification

## Summary
Green Globe is an international certification program that recognizes tourism businesses committed to environmental sustainability and operational benchmarking. In the Maldives, 46 member resorts hold Green Globe certification at varying levels (Platinum, Gold, and Certified), signaling their dedication to environmental stewardship and appeal to eco-conscious travelers.

## Key Facts
- 46 member resorts in the Maldives hold Green Globe certification
- Certification levels include Platinum, Gold, and Certified members
- Certification demonstrates sustainability commitment, environmental stewardship, and operational benchmarking
- Members range from luxury luxury resorts to mid-range properties across multiple atolls
- Some resorts are listed as members but have not yet completed final certification
- Green Globe certification appeals to and attracts eco-conscious travelers
