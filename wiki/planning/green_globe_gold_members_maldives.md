# Green Globe Gold Members Maldives

## Summary
Gold members represent a high tier of Green Globe certification in the Maldives, with demonstrated excellence in sustainability practices across hospitality operations.

## Key Facts
- Club Med Finolhu Villas holds Gold certification
- Club Med Kani holds Gold certification
- Grand Park Kodhipparu holds Gold certification
- OBLU NATURE Helengeli by SENTIDO holds Gold certification
- OZEN LIFE MAADHOO holds Gold certification
- OZEN RESERVE BOLIFUSHI holds Gold certification
- Gold is the second-highest certification tier in the Maldives
