# Green Globe Platinum Members Maldives

## Summary
Platinum members represent the highest tier of Green Globe certification in the Maldives. These resorts have demonstrated exceptional commitment to sustainability standards and environmental management practices.

## Key Facts
- Constance Halaveli Maldives is a Platinum member
- Constance Moofushi Maldives is a Platinum member
- Platinum is the highest certification tier awarded by Green Globe in the Maldives
