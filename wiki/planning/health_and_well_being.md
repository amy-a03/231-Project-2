# Health and Well-being

## Summary
Health is identified as an adaptation priority sector in the Maldives, addressing climate-related health risks including disease vectors, heat stress, and health system resilience in an island context.

## Key Facts
- Health is a designated adaptation priority sector
- Climate change impacts on health require resilience building
- Island geography presents unique health service delivery challenges
- Adaptation strategies support health system climate resilience
