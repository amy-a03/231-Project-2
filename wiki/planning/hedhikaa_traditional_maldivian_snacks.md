# Hedhikaa - Traditional Maldivian Snacks

## Summary
Hedhikaa represents traditional Maldivian snack foods that are part of local daily life and food culture. These foods are accessible to visitors staying in guest houses on inhabited islands.

## Key Facts
- Hedhikaa are traditional Maldivian snacks integral to local food culture
- Available through local tea shops and community dining when staying in guest houses
- Represents authentic local cuisine beyond resort-provided international food
