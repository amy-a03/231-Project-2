# Hope Spot

## Summary
A Hope Spot is a biologically significant area identified by Mission Blue as requiring urgent conservation attention and protection. Laamu Atoll in the Maldives is designated as the only atoll-level Hope Spot in the country, making it a priority conservation area.

## Key Facts
- Laamu Atoll is the only atoll-designated Hope Spot in the Maldives
- Designation indicates biological significance and conservation priority
- Surrounded by Marine Protected Areas
- Supports diverse marine megafauna including manta rays, whale sharks, and sea turtles
- Part of global network of critical marine conservation sites
