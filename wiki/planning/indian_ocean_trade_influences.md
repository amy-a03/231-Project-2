# Indian Ocean Trade Influences

## Summary
The Maldives has been shaped by centuries of trade and cultural exchange across the Indian Ocean, influencing language, arts, music, and traditions from East Africa, Arabia, and the Indian subcontinent.

## Key Facts
- Trade across the Indian Ocean has significantly influenced Maldivian culture
- Influences come from East Africa, Arabia, and the Indian subcontinent
- Cross-cultural exchanges shaped music, dance, and artistic traditions
- Historical trade connections remain visible in contemporary culture
