# Liveaboard Trip Preparation

## Summary
Successful liveaboard diving requires specific certifications, gear, and planning. Key recommendations include advanced certifications, specialized equipment, and comprehensive travel insurance.

## Key Facts
- Advanced Open Water and Nitrox certification recommended
- Reef-safe sunscreen required to protect marine ecosystems
- Motion sickness medication advised for sea comfort
- Soft-sided luggage preferred for boat storage constraints
- Dive-specific insurance with medical evacuation coverage essential
- Spare O-rings and batteries should be packed
- Arrive rested and hydrated before departure
