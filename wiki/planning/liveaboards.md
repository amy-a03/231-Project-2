# Liveaboards

## Summary
Liveaboards are floating resorts that offer cruise-based accommodation and diving experiences, with onboard alcohol availability and transfer access from Velana International Airport.

## Key Facts
- Liveaboards provide floating accommodation and diving opportunities
- Alcohol is available on liveaboard vessels
- Accessible via transfer from Velana International Airport
- Alternative to traditional resort island stays
