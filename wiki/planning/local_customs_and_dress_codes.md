# Local Customs and Dress Codes

## Summary
Visitors to the Maldives should observe Islamic customs, particularly on local islands. Dress codes are relaxed at resort islands but modest attire is expected in local communities.

## Key Facts
- Standard holiday clothing is acceptable at resorts
- Modest dress expected on local islands
- Bikinis are restricted and only permitted on designated beaches
- Respect for Islamic customs is important for cultural sensitivity
