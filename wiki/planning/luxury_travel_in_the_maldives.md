# Luxury Travel in the Maldives

## Summary
December through April is the premium season for luxury and honeymoon travel, offering picture-perfect weather, world-class resort stays, and the most reliable conditions for romantic escapes and upscale experiences.

## Key Facts
- December–April is the strongest period for luxury and honeymoon travel
- Peak season features picture-perfect weather and world-class resorts
- Early booking is essential during this high-demand period
- Calm seas and reliable sunshine enhance luxury resort experiences
