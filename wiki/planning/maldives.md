# Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives is a luxury destination known for pristine white-sand beaches, world-class underwater ecosystems, and high-end resorts. As a travel hub, it offers diverse attractions from cultural sites in the capital Male to natural marine environments across its atolls.

## Key Facts
- Major attractions in Male include the Old Friday Mosque, National Museum, Grand Friday Mosque, and the China-Maldives Friendship Bridge
- The destination features cultural sites such as Muleeaage & Medhu Ziyaarath and local markets including Fish Market and Produce Market
- Thilafushi is listed as a notable destination area
- Travel planning resources cover best times to visit, budget guidance, transportation, family travel, and accessibility
- The destination is characterized by luxury accommodations and underwater diving opportunities
