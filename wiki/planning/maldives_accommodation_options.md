# Maldives Accommodation Options

## Summary
The Maldives offers four primary accommodation categories serving different traveler types: luxury resorts, urban hotels, liveaboard vessels, and local guesthouses. Each provides distinct experiences from island resort living to authentic local community interaction.

## Key Facts
- 175 resorts ranging from club-like to boutique styles, including family-oriented options
- 16 hotels in Male convenient for business travelers
- 158 liveaboards enabling exploration of multiple locations with activity focus (e.g., surfing)
- 843 guesthouses offering local town stays and cultural immersion
- Total of 1,192 accommodation properties across all categories
