# Maldives Accommodation Overview

## Summary
The Maldives offers a wide range of accommodation options from budget guesthouses to luxury all-inclusive resorts distributed across multiple atolls and islands. Properties are widely available through booking platforms like Agoda, with amenities ranging from beachfront villas to overwater bungalows.

## Key Facts
- Accommodations span budget options (guesthouses on local islands like Maafushi) to ultra-luxury all-inclusive resorts
- Most resorts offer speedboat and seaplane transfer services from the main international airport
- Common guest priorities include beach quality, water clarity, food quality, and friendly staff
- Overwater villas are a signature accommodation type across multiple atolls
- Properties are distributed across South Ari Atoll, Baa Atoll, and other regions with varying price points
