# Maldives Adaptation Strategy

## Summary
The Maldives has developed comprehensive climate adaptation priorities focused on increasing resilience to climate hazards across multiple sectors. Key adaptation actions address coastal zones, infrastructure, tourism, fisheries, water resources, health, agriculture, and coral reef biodiversity, supported by national policies including the NAPA and Safer Island Strategy.

## Key Facts
- Adaptation is framed as a multi-dimensional goal supporting sustainable development
- Eight core adaptation priority sectors identified: coastal zones, infrastructure, tourism, fisheries, water resources, health, agriculture/food security, and coral reef biodiversity
- National Adaptation Programme of Action (NAPA) guides climate adaptation efforts
- Safer Island Strategy addresses vulnerability reduction
- Climate-resilient development programs and dedicated funds support implementation
- UNDP provides technical support for adaptation planning and execution
