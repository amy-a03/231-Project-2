# Maldives Diving

## Summary
The Maldives is a classic liveaboard diving destination known for channel dives, manta cleaning stations, and whale shark encounters. Its remote dive sites and rich marine biodiversity make it ideal for extended dive trips.

## Key Facts
- Channel dives are a signature diving experience
- Manta cleaning stations attract large populations of mantas
- Whale shark encounters are commonly featured on liveaboard itineraries
- Remote site accessibility makes liveaboard format especially suitable
- Perennial favorite for advanced and wildlife-focused diving
