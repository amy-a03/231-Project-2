# Maldives Independence Day

## Summary
The Maldives celebrates independence on July 26 with festivities throughout the country. This national holiday may include parades and performances as part of celebrations.

## Key Facts
- Independence Day date: July 26
- Celebrations include parades and performances
- Falls during the wet season with higher rain probability
- August following Independence Day remains a surprisingly popular travel month
