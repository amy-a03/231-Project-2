# Maldives Overview

## Summary
The Maldives is a destination combining beautiful islands, high-end travel experiences, and significant marine and environmental importance. As the world's lowest-lying islands, the nation faces critical climate threats while offering luxury tourism and diverse recreational opportunities.

## Key Facts
- The Maldives consists of the world's lowest-lying islands
- Faces significant climate and environmental threats due to sea level rise
- Known as the honeymoon capital of the world
- Offers high-end luxury travel experiences
- Features renowned scuba diving and marine activities
- Combines tourism with environmental and marine conservation significance
- Composed of 26 coral atolls and more than 1,200 islands
- Deep-sea diving visibility reaches up to 40 metres
- Features spectacular dive and snorkel sites throughout the atolls
- Hosts dozens of island resorts catering to various budgets and preferences
- Known for palm-fringed beaches and pristine ocean environments
- Popular as a beach extension destination following Sri Lanka visits
