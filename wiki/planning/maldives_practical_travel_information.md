# Maldives Practical Travel Information

## Summary
Essential travel logistics for the Maldives include Dhivehi as the local language (English widely spoken in tourism), Maldivian rufiyaa currency, and Velana International Airport as the main gateway. Alcohol is restricted to private resorts and liveaboards.

## Key Facts
- Local language is Dhivehi; English is widely used in tourism infrastructure
- Currency is Maldivian rufiyaa
- Velana International Airport serves as the primary international entry point
- Alcohol sales limited to private resorts and liveaboards only
- Seaplane transfers available for remote resorts, typically arranged by the resort
