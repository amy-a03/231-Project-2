# Maldives Seasonal Travel Guide

## Summary
The Maldives is a year-round destination with distinct seasonal patterns. Peak season (December–February) offers ideal weather and lively crowds but at premium prices, while off-season (May–November) provides lower costs and privacy with richer marine life, and shoulder season (March–April) balances both conditions.

## Key Facts
- Peak season runs December to February with ideal sunshine, gentle breezes, and excellent diving/snorkeling conditions
- Off-season (May–November) features lower prices, greater privacy, rich marine life, and good water conditions despite higher humidity
- Shoulder season (March–April) offers a balance of warm temperatures, tranquility, fewer crowds, and occasional rainfall
- Peak season experiences higher prices and larger crowds
- Off-season activities may be limited and humidity is elevated
- Shoulder season prices are higher than off-season but lower than peak
- Travel choice depends on priorities: weather, crowds, or price
