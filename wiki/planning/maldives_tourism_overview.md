# Maldives Tourism Overview

## Summary
The Maldives is an Indian Ocean island nation positioned as a premier global tourism destination under the slogan 'The Sunny Side of Life.' It has consistently won major international tourism awards and offers diverse accommodation and experience options.

## Key Facts
- Designated World's Leading Destination for 2020-2025 consecutively
- Awarded World's Leading Green Destination for 2024 and 2025
- Comprises 1,192 islands stretching across 871 kilometers
- Spans the equator in the Indian Ocean
- Populated for more than 2,500 years with ancient origins
