# Maldives Transfer Services

## Summary
Overview of private transfer options in the Maldives including domestic flights, seaplanes, speedboats, and ferries. Advance booking is essential as services are frequently fully booked on arrival day.

## Key Facts
- Four main transfer types available: domestic plane, seaplane, speedboat, and ferry
- Transfers are often fully booked on arrival day, requiring advance booking
- No public resort ferries exist; all resort transfers are private or operator-arranged
- Seaplane transfers operate only during daylight hours
- Same-day seaplane connections require arrival in Malé before mid-afternoon
- Flight details are required for proper transfer scheduling
- Late arrivals or missed transfers may result in no refund unless service provider cancels
