# Maldives Transportation Network

## Summary
Comprehensive overview of how travelers move within and to the Maldives, connecting international arrival with domestic distribution across atolls.

## Key Facts
- Multiple transport modes serve different distance and cost needs
- International flights arrive at Velana International Airport
- Domestic movement uses seaplanes, speedboats, domestic flights, and ferries
- Resort transfers organized by accommodations using speedboats or seaplanes
- Strong regional connectivity from South Asia, Middle East, and Europe
