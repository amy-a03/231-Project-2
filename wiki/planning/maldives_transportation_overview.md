# Maldives Transportation Overview

## Summary
The Maldives relies on a geography-dependent transport system where speedboats, seaplanes, and domestic flights connect the capital to resort atolls. Most visitors arrive at Velana International Airport and arrange transfers based on their resort's distance from the capital.

## Key Facts
- Speedboats are the primary transfer method for resorts near Velana International Airport
- Seaplanes and domestic flights serve resorts in distant atolls
- Scheduled ferries operate from Malé to many atolls as an alternative transport option
- Pre-arranged resort transfers are a common practice for arriving visitors
- Taxis are available from the airport for travel within Malé
