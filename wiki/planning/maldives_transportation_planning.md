# Maldives Transportation Planning

## Summary
Guide to planning transportation logistics for travel to the Maldives, including the importance of advance booking, flight scheduling coordination, and understanding transfer availability constraints.

## Key Facts
- Provide flight details in advance for proper transfer scheduling
- Book transfers well ahead as same-day availability is limited
- Understand cancellation and refund policies before booking
- Mid-afternoon arrival cutoff times apply for same-day seaplane connections
- Late flight arrivals may result in transfer service unavailability
