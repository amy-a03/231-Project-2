# Maldives Travel Practicalities

## Summary
Essential travel information for visiting the Maldives includes time zone, currency, visa requirements, weather considerations, and cultural expectations regarding clothing on inhabited islands.

## Key Facts
- Modest clothing is expected on inhabited islands
- Weather and monsoon seasons affect travel planning
- Visa requirements apply to international visitors
- Currency and tax information are essential for budgeting
