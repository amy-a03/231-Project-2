# Maldives Underwater Initiative

## Summary
A collaborative marine research and conservation program supported by Six Senses Laamu in partnership with the Manta Trust, Blue Marine Foundation, and Olive Ridley Project. The initiative focuses on marine science, guest education, community engagement, and habitat protection.

## Key Facts
- Partnership includes Manta Trust, Blue Marine Foundation, and Olive Ridley Project
- Supports marine research, guest education, community engagement, and habitat protection
- Conducts scientific surveys and megafauna monitoring
- Works on reef and seagrass protection initiatives
- Engages local communities in conservation efforts
