# Maldivian Arts and Crafts

## Summary
Maldivian arts include music, dance, and traditional crafts that reflect influences from East Africa, Arabia, and the Indian subcontinent. Traditional craftsmanship includes lacquered wooden ornaments, woven reed mats, and coral carvings.

## Key Facts
- Maldivian music and dance reflect influences from East Africa, Arabia, and the Indian subcontinent
- Traditional crafts include lacquered wooden ornaments
- Woven reed mats are a significant craft tradition
- Coral carvings represent traditional artistic heritage
- Arts and crafts demonstrate cultural exchanges across the Indian Ocean
