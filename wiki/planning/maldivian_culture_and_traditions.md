# Maldivian Culture and Traditions

## Summary
Maldivian culture is shaped by island life, maritime heritage, and influences from communities and trade across the Indian Ocean. It encompasses distinct language, traditional practices, arts, and customs.

## Key Facts
- Maldivian culture is shaped by island life and the surrounding sea
- Culture has been influenced by Indian Ocean trade and cross-cultural exchanges
- Traditions include language, maritime heritage, cuisine, arts, and crafts
- Cultural customs and dress codes reflect local traditions and values
- Understanding culture is essential for respectful sustainable travel
