# Maldivian Currency and Payments

## Summary
The Maldivian rufiyaa is the official currency, though US dollars are widely accepted at resorts and tourist facilities throughout the country.

## Key Facts
- Maldivian rufiyaa is the local currency
- US dollars widely accepted at resorts and tourist areas
- Dual currency system accommodates international visitors
- Resort islands typically use USD for convenience
