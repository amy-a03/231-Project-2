# Maldivian Hospitality

## Summary
Maldivian hospitality is a defining cultural trait rooted in welcoming visitors and sharing meals and experiences. This tradition extends across all accommodation types—resorts, guesthouses, hotels, and liveaboards—and is particularly visible in local-island stays where guests can dine and interact directly with residents.

## Key Facts
- Hospitality is a core cultural value expressed through welcomes, shared meals, and invitations into local life
- Boduberu music is part of the hospitality experience, often accompanying social gatherings
- Guesthouses and hotels on local islands offer the most authentic hospitality experiences, including dining with locals
- Homestay-style accommodations provide direct immersion in local hospitality traditions
- Hospitality culture is not limited to resorts but exists across all accommodation types in the Maldives
