# Maldivian Local Cuisine

## Summary
Maldivian cuisine features distinctive regional dishes that vary across the archipelago while maintaining core ingredients and flavors. Local food is readily available on inhabited islands and select resorts, offering visitors an authentic culinary experience beyond standard resort buffets.

## Key Facts
- Regional specialties include Addu Bon'di, Naroh Falidha, Golha Riha, Muran'ga Baiy, Haalu Folhi, Masbon'di, and Kashekiyo Foah
- Traditional beverages include toddy, Dhiyaa Hakuru, and Karu Hakuru, each with distinct preparation methods
- Local food is accessible on inhabited islands and available at select resorts
- Regional variations reflect the geography and local ingredients of different atolls
- Authentic local dining provides an alternative to standard resort buffet experiences
