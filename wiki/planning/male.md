# Male

## Summary
Male is the capital city of the Maldives and serves as the cultural and administrative hub of the nation. It contains several important historical mosques, museums, and markets that provide insight into local life and heritage.

## Key Facts
- Old Friday Mosque is a key cultural attraction in Male
- National Museum of Maldives is located in the capital
- Grand Friday Mosque is a major religious and architectural site
- China-Maldives Friendship Bridge is an important infrastructure landmark
- Fish Market and Produce Market offer authentic local experiences
