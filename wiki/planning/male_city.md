# Male City

## Summary
Male is the capital city of the Maldives and serves as a business and urban accommodation hub for travelers. It hosts tourism industry facilities and government institutions.

## Key Facts
- Capital of the Maldives
- Contains 16 hotels for business visitors and urban travelers
- Host location for tourism industry briefings and events
- Live temperature recorded at 32.02°C as of April 2026
