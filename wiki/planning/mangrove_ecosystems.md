# Mangrove Ecosystems

## Summary
Mangroves in the Maldives are nature-based solutions for climate adaptation, providing coastal protection, fish nursery habitat, and carbon storage. Their conservation and restoration support broader reef resilience strategies.

## Key Facts
- Mangroves are part of nature-based adaptation solutions in Maldives
- They provide fish nursery habitat supporting fisheries productivity
- Mangrove protection helps stabilize coastal areas against rising seas
- Combined reef-mangrove systems enhance overall ecosystem resilience
- Restoration efforts should be integrated with reef conservation
