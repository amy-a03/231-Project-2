# Monsoon Seasons in Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives experiences monsoon weather patterns that affect rainfall and travel conditions throughout the year. The southwest monsoon (June–August) brings the heaviest rain, while the northeast monsoon period (November–April) offers the driest conditions.

## Key Facts
- Southwest monsoon (June–August): heaviest rainfall, sudden downpours that are typically short-lived
- Northeast monsoon period (November–April): driest conditions with warm temperatures around 30°C
- Transition months (May, September–October): moderate rainfall, fewer tourists, better value accommodations
- Strong equatorial sun year-round requires consistent sun protection regardless of season
