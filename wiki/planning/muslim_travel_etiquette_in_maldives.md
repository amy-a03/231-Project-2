# Muslim Travel Etiquette in Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives is a Muslim country with specific social and cultural norms that travelers, especially on local islands, must respect. These include dress codes, alcohol restrictions, and awareness of religious observances.

## Key Facts
- Dress modestly on local islands, avoiding revealing clothing
- Public swimwear should be confined to designated beach areas
- Alcohol is not sold on local islands
- Friday ferry schedules are affected by Islamic observances
- Resort islands have more relaxed policies compared to local islands
