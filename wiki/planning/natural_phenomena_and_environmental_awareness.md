# Natural Phenomena and Environmental Awareness

## Summary
The Maldives is known for natural wonders like bioluminescent sea phenomena, but visitors should have realistic expectations about seeing these natural occurrences.

## Key Facts
- The sea of stars is a natural bioluminescent phenomenon
- Bioluminescent displays cannot be guaranteed on any particular night
- Weather conditions affect visibility of natural phenomena
- Reef-friendly sunscreen should be used to protect marine ecosystems
- Environmental awareness is important for sustainable travel
