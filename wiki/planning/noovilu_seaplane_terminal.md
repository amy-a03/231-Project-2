# Noovilu Seaplane Terminal

## Summary
The Noovilu seaplane terminal is a dedicated facility at Velana International Airport designed to handle seaplane transfers between the airport and island resorts throughout the Maldives.

## Key Facts
- Located at Velana International Airport
- Dedicated terminal facility for seaplane operations
- Primary transfer method for reaching resorts across the atolls
- Part of the airport's comprehensive transportation infrastructure
