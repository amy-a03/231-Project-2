# Off-Season (May–November)

## Summary
The off-season in the Maldives spans May through November and appeals to travelers seeking solitude, budget savings, and abundant marine life, though characterized by higher humidity and occasional activity limitations.

## Key Facts
- Runs May to November
- Lower accommodation and flight prices
- Greater privacy and serene atmosphere
- Rich marine life and biodiversity
- Good snorkeling and diving conditions
- Higher humidity levels
- Some water activities and excursions may be limited
