# Packing and Import Restrictions

## Summary
Travelers to the Maldives should pack appropriate items for the tropical climate while being aware of restricted imports that reflect local religious and cultural values.

## Key Facts
- Lightweight clothing is essential for the tropical climate
- Reef-friendly sunscreen is recommended for water activities
- Mosquito repellent should be included in packing
- Alcohol is a restricted import in the Maldives
- Pork products are restricted imports due to Islamic dietary laws
