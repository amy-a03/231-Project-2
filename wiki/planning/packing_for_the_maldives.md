# Packing for the Maldives

## Summary
A practical guide to essential items for a Maldives trip, balancing beach comfort with protection from sun, water, and travel inconveniences. Covers both resort stays and local island visits.

## Key Facts
- Sunscreen and sun-protective clothing are critical due to intense tropical sun exposure
- Sunglasses, insect repellent, and motion-sickness remedies address common travel challenges
- Reusable water bottle and electrolyte tablets help with hydration in the hot climate
- Essential medications, universal travel adapter, and travel documents are non-negotiable
- Camera and power banks enable documentation of activities and extended device use
- Waterproof cases protect electronics and valuables from water exposure during water activities
- Comfortable clothing and swimwear should be prioritized for the warm, relaxed environment
- Travel insurance is recommended for comprehensive trip coverage
