# Peak Season (December–February)

## Summary
The peak tourism season in the Maldives spans December through February, characterized by sunny skies, calm seas, and bustling resort atmospheres, but with correspondingly higher prices and larger crowds.

## Key Facts
- Runs December to February
- Ideal weather with sunshine and blue skies
- Gentle sea breezes and calm conditions
- Excellent for snorkeling, scuba diving, and surfing
- Lively and bustling atmosphere at resorts
- Significantly higher accommodation and flight prices
- Larger crowds and reduced privacy
