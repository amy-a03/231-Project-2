# Rainy Season (May-November)

## Summary
The rainy season from May to November features warm weather with heavy but brief showers, reduced diving visibility due to plankton, and increased marine life activity including turtle nesting and manta ray presence.

## Key Facts
- Rainfall peaks in September and October with heavy downpours possible
- Showers are typically short-lived despite being intense
- Plankton increases during this period, reducing visibility but attracting manta rays and whale sharks
- May and June feature sea turtle nesting on beaches
- Turtle hatchlings emerge around July and August
- Lower prices on flights and resorts during this season
