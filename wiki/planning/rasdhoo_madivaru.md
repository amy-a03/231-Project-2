# Rasdhoo Madivaru

## Summary
An oceanic reef site in the Maldives that demonstrated stable coral cover over 19 years of monitoring, serving as a key research location for understanding coral resilience in minimally impacted reef environments.

## Key Facts
- Oceanic reef site in the Maldives
- Maintained stable coral cover across the 19-year study period
- Exhibited minimal human impact compared to other monitored sites
- Used as a baseline for understanding natural reef recovery patterns
