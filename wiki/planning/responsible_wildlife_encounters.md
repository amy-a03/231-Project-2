# Responsible Wildlife Encounters

## Summary
Guidelines for responsible whale shark encounters in the Maldives establish safe distances and behavioral protocols to protect both visitors and marine wildlife. These standards minimize stress and injury risk during interactions.

## Key Facts
- Maintain minimum 3 metres distance from shark body
- Maintain minimum 4 metres distance from shark tail
- Never touch the shark
- Do not obstruct the shark's movement
- Enter water calmly and from a distance
