# Scuba Diving in Maldives

## Summary
Scuba diving is a prominent travel experience in the Maldives, featured prominently in travel guides and tourism marketing. The islands' marine environment supports diverse diving opportunities.

## Key Facts
- Scuba diving is a major draw for tourism to the Maldives
- Featured in travel planning resources and guides
- Part of the broader marine tourism experience
- Accessible to travelers seeking underwater exploration
