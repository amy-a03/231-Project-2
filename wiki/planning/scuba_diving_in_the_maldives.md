# Scuba Diving in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives is a premier scuba diving destination known for crystal-clear warm waters, abundant marine life including manta rays and whale sharks, and diverse accommodation options from guesthouses to liveaboards. Waters remain warm year-round in the low to mid-80s Fahrenheit, making it accessible for divers of various skill levels.

## Key Facts
- Water temperature stays in the low to mid-80s Fahrenheit year-round
- Best marine life encounters occur May through November in Baa, Ari, and North Mal areas
- South Ari Marine Park offers consistent year-round whale shark and manta ray sightings
- Over 180 resorts available with options ranging from guesthouses to high-end facilities
- Liveaboards provide lodging, food, and dive coordination while allowing flexibility to follow conditions
- Seaplane transfers to some resorts cost several hundred dollars round-trip and operate only during daylight
