# Sea-Level Rise Projections

## Summary
The World Bank climate profile presents projections showing potential sea-level rise exceeding 0.5 meters by 2100 under higher emissions scenarios, with major implications for the Maldives' survival as a nation.

## Key Facts
- Sea-level rise projections extend to 2100 under multiple emissions scenarios
- Higher emissions scenarios show potential rise exceeding 0.5 meters
- A 1-meter rise would eliminate the entire land area of the Maldives
- Projections include rising temperatures and uncertain precipitation changes
- Sea-level rise is coupled with storm surge and coastal flooding risks
