# Sea of Stars Phenomenon

## Summary
The Maldives exhibits a bioluminescent phenomenon where glowing plankton create a 'Sea of Stars' effect along the shoreline at night. This natural spectacle is most visible during specific seasonal windows and lunar conditions.

## Key Facts
- Caused by bioluminescent plankton that glow blue along the shoreline
- Best viewing window: late summer through end of year (approximately June–December)
- Famous viewing location: Vaadhoo Island
- Optimal viewing conditions occur on new moon nights when darkness is greatest
- Provides a unique and romantic nighttime experience
