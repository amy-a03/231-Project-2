# Seagrass Conservation

## Summary
Seagrass meadows in the Maldives are protected as critical marine habitat and a conservation priority. Six Senses Laamu works to safeguard these ecosystems, which serve as nurseries for fish and support biodiversity.

## Key Facts
- 335,024 square meters of seagrass meadows protected as of 2024
- Seagrass identified as important habitat and conservation priority
- Serves as nursery habitat for marine species
- Part of integrated coastal and marine protection strategy
- Monitored through scientific surveys and research programs
