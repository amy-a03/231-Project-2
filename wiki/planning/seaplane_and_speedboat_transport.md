# Seaplane and Speedboat Transport

## Summary
Seaplane and speedboat transfers are the primary transportation methods connecting Maldives airports to resort islands and local islands, essential for island access across the archipelago.

## Key Facts
- Speedboat transfers are standard for nearby islands and budget accommodations
- Seaplane transfers service more distant atolls and luxury resorts
- Most accommodation properties offer transfer coordination as part of booking
- Critical infrastructure for accessing the geographically dispersed island accommodations
