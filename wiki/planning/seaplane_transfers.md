# Seaplane Transfers

## Summary
Seaplane transfers are a primary transportation method for reaching island resorts in the Maldives from Velana International Airport, operating from the dedicated Noovilu seaplane terminal.

## Key Facts
- Operated from Noovilu seaplane terminal at the airport
- Direct transfer service to resorts across multiple atolls
- Scenic and efficient alternative to boat transfers
- Well-organized transfer point as part of airport logistics
