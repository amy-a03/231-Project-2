# Seaplane Transport

## Summary
Seaplane transfers are a primary transport method for reaching remote atolls in the Maldives, operating exclusively during daylight hours with strict timing constraints for same-day connections.

## Key Facts
- Seaplanes operate only during daylight hours
- Same-day seaplane transfers require arrival in Malé before mid-afternoon
- Often fully booked on arrival day
- Advance booking is essential for guaranteed service
- Seaplanes operate from Velana International Airport
- Direct service to Ari Atoll resorts
- Premium transport option with faster transit times
