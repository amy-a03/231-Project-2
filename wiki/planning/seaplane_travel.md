# Seaplane Travel

## Summary
Primary transfer method for resorts located farther from Velana International Airport, offering scenic aerial routes over atolls and reefs.

## Key Facts
- Standard transfer method for distant resorts from main airport
- Enables direct access to remote resort locations
- More commonly used than speedboats for resorts beyond speedboat range
- Provides scenic views of atolls and marine environment during transfer
