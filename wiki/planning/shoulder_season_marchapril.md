# Shoulder Season (March–April)

## Summary
The shoulder season in March and April represents a transitional period between peak and off-season, offering a balance of pleasant weather, reduced crowds, and moderate pricing.

## Key Facts
- Spans March and April
- Tranquil atmosphere with fewer crowds than peak season
- Warm temperatures ideal for outdoor activities
- Occasional rainfall during transition
- Balanced experience between extremes
- Prices higher than off-season but lower than peak season
- Good alternative for travelers seeking weather without peak-season costs
