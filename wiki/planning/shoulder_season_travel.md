# Shoulder Season Travel

## Summary
May and October represent the most affordable travel months to the Maldives, offering significantly reduced hotel prices while still providing favorable conditions for visitors. These shoulder months are recommended for budget-conscious travelers seeking cost savings without sacrificing comfort.

## Key Facts
- May and October are identified as the best budget months for visiting the Maldives
- Shoulder season travel reduces hotel prices significantly compared to peak seasons
- These months fall between major tourist seasons, offering lower accommodation rates
