# Shoulder Seasons

## Summary
Transition periods in late October and late April offering a balance between pleasant weather and lower accommodation costs. These windows provide opportunities to avoid peak crowds while maintaining good conditions.

## Key Facts
- Occur in late October and late April
- Offer lower hotel room rates and reduced flight prices to Male
- Feature average temperatures around 88°F
- Provide pleasant weather conditions with fewer tourists than peak season
- Ideal for budget-conscious travelers seeking good-value stays
