# Snorkeling and Diving Conditions

## Summary
Water visibility and marine conditions vary significantly by season and location. The dry season and specific atolls provide optimal underwater visibility for snorkeling and diving.

## Key Facts
- Eastern atolls offer the best visibility for underwater activities
- Dry season (November to April) provides strong visibility due to northeast-flowing currents
- Summer months (May to October) offer excellent diving conditions despite rain
- Whale sharks and manta rays are most commonly spotted between August and November
- Water remains warm year-round supporting diverse marine life
