# Solo Travel to Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives caters to solo travelers despite being marketed as the honeymoon capital. Solo travel experiences to the islands are featured in travel guides and tourism content.

## Key Facts
- Solo travel is a viable and marketed travel segment for the Maldives
- Positioned alongside the destination's honeymoon reputation
- Featured in travel planning guides and recommendations
