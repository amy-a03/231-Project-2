# Speedboat Transport

## Summary
Speedboats are a primary transport option for reaching Ari Atoll from the main airport, available in both private and shared configurations.

## Key Facts
- Speedboats operate from Velana International Airport to Ari Atoll
- Both private and shared speedboat options are available
- Speedboats offer a mid-range option between faster seaplanes and slower ferries
