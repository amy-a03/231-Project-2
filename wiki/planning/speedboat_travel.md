# Speedboat Travel

## Summary
Fast boat transfer service used for resorts located closer to Velana International Airport, providing cost-effective atoll connectivity.

## Key Facts
- Primary transfer method for resorts near the main airport
- Less expensive alternative to seaplane transfers
- Limited to resorts within reasonable distance from Velana International Airport
- Part of standard resort transfer arrangements
