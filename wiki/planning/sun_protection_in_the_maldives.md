# Sun Protection in the Maldives

## Summary
Critical information about protecting against the intense tropical sun in the Maldives, essential for all visitors to prevent sunburn and heat-related issues.

## Key Facts
- Sunscreen is a packing priority due to the Maldives' intense equatorial sun exposure
- Sun-protective clothing (rash guards, long-sleeve shirts) provides additional UV protection
- Sunglasses are essential for eye protection from UV rays and water glare
- The tropical climate requires consistent sun protection throughout the day
- Sun protection is particularly important for water-based activities and beach time
