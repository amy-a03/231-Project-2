# Surfing

## Summary
The Maldives offers excellent surfing conditions with distinct regional and seasonal variations, with southern atolls receiving the best early-season swell in March and larger swells arriving in July across the islands.

## Key Facts
- March marks the start of surf season with southern atolls seeing the best early-season swell
- Central and northern atolls become better for surfing later in the wet season
- July offers larger swells, making it ideal for surfing
- Surfing conditions improve during the rainy season (May-November)
