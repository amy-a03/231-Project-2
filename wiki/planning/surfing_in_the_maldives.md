# Surfing in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives offers excellent surfing conditions during specific seasons, with bigger waves and choppier water ideal for surfers seeking challenging conditions. Multiple seasonal windows cater to different skill levels and preferences.

## Key Facts
- May to October is best for bigger waves and choppier water
- February through April can also bring good swells with less rain risk
- Wet season provides stronger surf conditions
