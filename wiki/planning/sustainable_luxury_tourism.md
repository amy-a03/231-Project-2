# Sustainable Luxury Tourism

## Summary
An operational framework demonstrated by Four Seasons Maldives that integrates environmental stewardship, conservation, and community benefit into high-end resort management.

## Key Facts
- Phasing out single-use plastics in food and beverage operations
- Recycled or sustainably sourced dive-shop items and equipment
- Local and ethical procurement practices prioritized
- Third-party sustainability benchmarking and certification tracked
- Ongoing staff training ensures environmental standards are maintained across all operations
