# Sustainable Tourism in Maldives

## Summary
Sustainability is central to Maldives tourism positioning, with official recognition as a leading green destination and individual resorts achieving international environmental certifications.

## Key Facts
- World's Leading Green Destination award for 2024 and 2025
- Grand Park Kodhipparu Maldives achieved 7th consecutive Green Globe Certification
- Resorts pursuing Gold Member status for sustainable practices
- Focus on island hospitality and environmental stewardship
- 'Foster Our Fehurihi' campaign emphasizes environmental care
