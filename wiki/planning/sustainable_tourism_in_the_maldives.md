# Sustainable Tourism in the Maldives

## Summary
Tourism is a major economic driver in the Maldives but creates direct pressures on coral reef ecosystems through dredging, coastal development, and marine activities. Balancing economic needs with reef conservation is critical for long-term sustainability.

## Key Facts
- Tourism development requires dredging and coastal modification
- Tourism-related activities directly damage reef communities
- Tourism pressures reduce ecosystem resilience to climate stress
- Long-term reef viability depends on sustainable tourism practices
