# Sustainable Tourism Maldives

## Summary
Sustainable tourism in the Maldives encompasses guesthouse development, local community engagement, and practices that distribute tourism benefits beyond luxury resort enclaves.

## Key Facts
- Guesthouses support local tourism income distribution
- Budget accommodation options reduce environmental footprint per visitor
- Local island tourism preserves community character and economic self-determination
- Activities like traditional fishing provide cultural exchange and local employment
