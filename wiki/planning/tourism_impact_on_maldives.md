# Tourism Impact on Maldives

## Summary
Tourism development in the Maldives directly influences local fishing practices and reef ecosystem pressure through increased resort demand for fresh fish. This creates both economic opportunities and conservation challenges.

## Key Facts
- Resorts purchase fresh fish directly from local fishermen
- Tourism growth increases demand for local fish resources
- Direct supply relationships link resort operations to fishing pressure
- Economic benefits must be balanced against ecosystem sustainability concerns
