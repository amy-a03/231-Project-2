# Tourism Seasons

## Summary
The Maldives has distinct peak and low seasons affecting prices, crowds, and available activities. Peak season (December-April) offers perfect weather but higher costs, while low season (May-November) provides budget-friendly options with unique wildlife experiences.

## Key Facts
- Peak season (December-April) requires advance resort and flight bookings
- Low season (May-November) offers significantly cheaper flights and resort prices
- January is the busiest month with highest demand
- September-October are quieter periods with substantially reduced costs
