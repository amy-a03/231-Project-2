# Traditional Maldivian Boat (Dhoni)

## Summary
The dhoni is a traditional Maldivian fishing and transport vessel refined over centuries to suit local sea conditions. It represents an important part of maritime heritage and island life.

## Key Facts
- The dhoni is a traditional Maldivian boat refined over centuries
- Design is specifically adapted to local sea conditions
- Represents core maritime heritage of island communities
- Integral to traditional fishing and transport practices
