# Velana International Airport

## Summary
Velana International Airport (formerly Male International Airport) is the primary aviation hub for the Maldives, serving as the main entry point for international travelers and the gateway for domestic transfers to resorts and other atolls.

## Key Facts
- Main airport hub for the Maldives
- Features a dedicated seaplane terminal for inter-atoll transfers
- Includes a domestic terminal for flights to other atolls
- Provides taxi access to nearby Male and Hulhumalé
- Serves as the starting point for speedboat and resort transfers
- Critical infrastructure for connecting international arrivals to scattered resort islands
- Features a new international terminal for enhanced passenger facilities
- Operates the Noovilu seaplane terminal for seaplane transfers to resorts
- Provides multiple transfer options: resort transfers, speedboats, seaplanes, ferries, and domestic flights
- Offers airport services including Wi-Fi, dining, and lounges
- Connects to domestic airports across the archipelago for inter-island travel
- International flights arrive at Velana International Airport
- Onward transfers available by speedboat or seaplane based on destination distance
- Late arrivals may require overnight stay in Malé before onward transfer
- Hub for accessing resort islands and liveaboards
- Main gateway for international arrivals to the Maldives
- Receives regular flights from Europe, Middle East, and South/Southeast Asia
- Strong connectivity from Sri Lanka, India, and Dubai
- Serves as the hub for domestic connections to atolls
- Most visitors to the Maldives arrive through Velana International Airport
- Airport offers taxi services for travel to Malé
- Hub point for arranging onward transfers to resort atolls
