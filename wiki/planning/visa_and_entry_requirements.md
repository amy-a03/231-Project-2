# Visa and Entry Requirements

## Summary
The Maldives offers visa on arrival for most nationalities, valid for 30 days. Travelers must complete the IMUGA traveler declaration online before arrival and are subject to restrictions on certain items.

## Key Facts
- Visa on arrival available for most nationalities for 30 days
- IMUGA traveler declaration must be completed online before arrival
- Alcohol, pork products, and certain religious items are restricted for import
- Standard processing allows entry without advance visa applications
