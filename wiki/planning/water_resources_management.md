# Water Resources Management

## Summary
Water resources management is a critical adaptation priority for the Maldives, addressing challenges of freshwater scarcity, saltwater intrusion, and the need for climate-resilient water supply systems.

## Key Facts
- Water resources is a designated adaptation priority sector
- Freshwater intrusion is a primary climate hazard threatening supply
- Islands require innovative water management solutions
- Adaptation strategies aim to ensure sustainable freshwater availability
