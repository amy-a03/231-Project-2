# Water Sports Activities

## Summary
The Maldives offers diverse water sports throughout the year, with optimal conditions during dry season months and specific activities suited to different seasons.

## Key Facts
- Paddle boarding is recommended in January
- Parasailing offers aerial island views and is recommended in March
- July offers good surfing conditions with average swells of 2 to 8 feet
- October is the last good month for surfing as monsoon conditions ease
- Seaplane flights provide scenic experiences, particularly in April
- Diving, snorkeling, and water activities are best during December through April
