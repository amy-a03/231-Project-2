# Water Sports and Activities

## Summary
The Maldives offers diverse water activities including snorkeling, kayaking, diving, and surfing, each with optimal seasons. December and January provide the calmest waters for snorkeling and kayaking, while other seasons favor different activities.

## Key Facts
- Snorkeling and kayaking are best in December and January for calmer water conditions
- Diving optimal January through April
- Surfing best May to October with alternative season February through April
- Different water activities peak at different times throughout the year
