# Water Sports in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives supports diverse water sports throughout the year. Kitesurfing and windsurfing are strongest from May–August due to strong winds, while snorkeling and diving peak January–April with optimal visibility.

## Key Facts
- May–August brings stronger winds suitable for kitesurfing and windsurfing
- Snorkeling and diving have optimal conditions January–April
- Water sports opportunities vary seasonally based on wind and visibility conditions
- Both monsoon and dry seasons support active water recreation
