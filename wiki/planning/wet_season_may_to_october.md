# Wet Season (May to October)

## Summary
The monsoon period bringing higher humidity, rain showers, and occasional strong winds. However, it offers low-season rates, excellent diving conditions, and opportunities to witness turtle nesting.

## Key Facts
- Runs from May to October with peak monsoon effects June through August
- Short-lived rain showers often followed by clear skies
- Offers substantial hotel discounts and low-season rates
- Features excellent diving and surfing conditions despite rainfall
- Best time to observe turtle nests hatching
