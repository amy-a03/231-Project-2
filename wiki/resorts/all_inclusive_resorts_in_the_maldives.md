# All-Inclusive Resorts in the Maldives

## Summary
All-inclusive resorts in the Maldives bundle meals, drinks, activities, and often transfers or spa services, offering a simplified travel experience after the long journey to Malé. Travel + Leisure recommends nine award-winning properties that cater to different priorities: romance, activity, and sustainability.

## Key Facts
- All-inclusive packages typically include meals, drinks, activities, and sometimes transfers or spa perks
- Kudadoo Maldives is the country's first entirely solar-powered resort
- Popular amenities include private pool villas, butler service, underwater dining, and marine wildlife encounters
- Resorts offer specialized experiences such as whale shark spotting, manta ray diving, dolphin cruises, and 360-degree house reefs
- Pack reef-safe sunscreen due to strong equatorial UV
- Avoid packing alcohol and pork products, which are prohibited
- Splitting time across multiple islands is recommended for added variety
