# Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru

## Summary
A luxury resort in the Maldives that operates with sustainability as a core brand value. The property holds EarthCheck Gold Certification and implements programs for reef restoration, plastic reduction, and environmental monitoring.

## Key Facts
- Located in the Maldives with continuous EarthCheck certification since 2012, upgraded to Gold Certification in 2022
- Measures annual sustainability indicators including energy, emissions, water, waste, paper use, chemical/pesticide use, and community involvement
- Operates a coral rope nursery with 1,000 coral fragments transplanted per nursery in 2016
- Built an electric reef on its house reef in 2001, part of Maldives reef restoration history
- Achieved 75% reduction in single-use plastic usage through initiatives begun in 2018
