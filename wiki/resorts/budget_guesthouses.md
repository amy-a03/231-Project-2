# Budget Guesthouses

## Summary
Budget guesthouses on local islands like Maafushi provide affordable accommodation for budget-conscious travelers, offering basic to mid-range amenities at significantly lower prices than resort islands.

## Key Facts
- Located primarily on accessible local islands such as Maafushi
- Examples include Kuredhi Beach Inn, Arena Beach Hotel, and Narnia Maldives Hotel
- Offer direct beach access and interaction with local island culture
- More affordable alternative to private resort islands while maintaining reasonable comfort standards
