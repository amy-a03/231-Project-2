# Budget Resorts in the Maldives

## Summary
A category of lower-priced resort accommodations in the Maldives that offer resort-style amenities and services at more accessible price points than ultra-luxury properties. These resorts are ranked by traveler reviews on TripAdvisor based on value and guest satisfaction.

## Key Facts
- Budget resorts still offer relaxing resort experiences with snorkeling access and coral reef proximity
- Common amenities include welcoming staff, spa facilities, meals, and boat trip packages
- Popular budget options include Sun Siyam Iru Fushi, Niva Kurumba Maldives, Bandos Maldives, and Embudu Village
- Rankings are community-driven, based on TripAdvisor traveler reviews and ratings
- Suitable for budget-conscious families and couples seeking value accommodations
- Many properties offer package deals that bundle meals, spa credits, and excursions to improve value
