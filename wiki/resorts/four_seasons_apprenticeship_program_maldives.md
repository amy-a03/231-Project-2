# Four Seasons Apprenticeship Program Maldives

## Summary
A workforce development initiative that has trained over 850 young Maldivians in hospitality and sustainable practices, supporting local economic development.

## Key Facts
- More than 850 young Maldivians trained through the program
- Focuses on hospitality and sustainability skills development
- Part of Four Seasons' broader community engagement strategy
- Supports local employment and economic opportunity
- Integrated with resort operations at both Landaa Giraavaru and Kuda Huraa
