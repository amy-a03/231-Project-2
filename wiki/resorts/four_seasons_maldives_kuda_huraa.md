# Four Seasons Maldives Kuda Huraa

## Summary
A luxury resort partnering with Landaa Giraavaru in a coordinated approach to sustainable luxury operations, featuring marine conservation and community engagement programs.

## Key Facts
- EarthCheck Silver certified, working toward Gold certification
- Part of Four Seasons' two-island sustainable luxury operation in the Maldives
- Supports Marine Discovery Centre operations shared with Landaa Giraavaru
- Integrated into local procurement and staff training initiatives
- Promotes reef-safe sunscreen and sustainable seafood sourcing
