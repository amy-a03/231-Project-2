# Four Seasons Maldives Landaa Giraavaru

## Summary
A luxury resort in the Maldives that combines high-end hospitality with extensive environmental conservation programs, including coral restoration and marine education initiatives.

## Key Facts
- EarthCheck Silver certified, working toward Gold certification
- Part of Four Seasons' two-island sustainable luxury operation in the Maldives
- Home to a Marine Discovery Centre focused on coral propagation, turtle releases, and citizen science
- Contributed to planting more than 500,000 coral fragments since 2001
- Rescued and rehabilitated more than 280 sea turtles since program inception
