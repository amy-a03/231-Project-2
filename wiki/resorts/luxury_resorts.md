# Luxury Resorts

## Summary
Luxury all-inclusive resorts in the Maldives feature overwater villas, premium dining, and comprehensive amenities targeting high-end travelers seeking exclusive experiences.

## Key Facts
- Notable luxury properties include Baglioni Resort Maldives, Constance Moofushi, Emerald Faarufushi Resort & Spa, and Hard Rock Hotel Maldives
- Typically offer all-inclusive meal plans and water sports packages
- Feature overwater or beachfront villa accommodations
- Emphasize premium service quality and curated guest experiences
