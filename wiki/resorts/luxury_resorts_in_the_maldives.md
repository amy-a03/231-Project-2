# Luxury Resorts in the Maldives

## Summary
The Maldives hosts a curated collection of ultra-premium resort properties spanning multiple atolls, ranging from private island experiences to multi-villa complexes. Forbes Travel Guide indexes over 30 luxury and wellness-focused properties, many certified for responsible hospitality practices.

## Key Facts
- Major luxury resort brands include Four Seasons, One&Only, Soneva, Conrad, Anantara, Banyan Tree, Jumeirah, and The Ritz-Carlton
- Notable properties include Soneva Fushi, Kudadoo Maldives Private Island, Conrad Maldives Rangali Island, and One&Only Reethi Rah
- Many resorts carry 'Responsible Hospitality Verified' certification emphasizing conscious luxury and environmental harmony
- Properties span multiple atolls including Kuda Huraa, Landaa Giraavaru, South Ari Atoll, Fari Islands, and others
- Resort amenities commonly include spas, marine experiences, and eco-tourism activities
- Forbes Travel Guide serves as a directory index of curated luxury and wellness-oriented properties rather than detailed reviews
