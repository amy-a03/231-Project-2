# Maldives Guesthouses

## Summary
Guesthouses represent a shift from resort-only tourism in the Maldives, providing budget-friendly accommodation on local islands and enabling travelers to experience authentic island communities. They have democratized access to the Maldives beyond luxury resorts while supporting local economies.

## Key Facts
- Guesthouses significantly lower the cost of staying in the Maldives compared to resort-only options
- Travelers stay in working local communities with active fishing, schools, and local cafés
- Alcohol is forbidden on local islands; modest dress is required
- Common organized activities include snorkeling, diving, traditional fishing, sandbank visits, and island hopping
- Beaches on local islands are public rather than resort-managed, with designated bikini beaches now common
- Guesthouses have made the Maldives accessible to budget travelers, though transportation and excursions can still be expensive
