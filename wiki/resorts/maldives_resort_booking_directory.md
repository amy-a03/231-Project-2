# Maldives Resort Booking Directory

## Summary
Agoda's Maldive Islands page serves as a booking-oriented directory listing popular resorts, guesthouses, and hotels with guest reviews and satisfaction scores. The page emphasizes all-inclusive stays, transfer options, and popularity rankings across islands and atolls.

## Key Facts
- Agoda directory covers both luxury resorts and guesthouses across Maldives
- Properties listed include Meeru Maldives, Grand Park Kodhipparu, Dusit Thani Maldives, Huvafen Fushi, Velassaru Maldives, and Anantara Veli
- Guest reviews focus on transfers, service quality, beaches, food, and overwater villa experiences
- All-inclusive and transfer-inclusive stay options are highlighted
- Resorts are organized by island and atoll popularity
