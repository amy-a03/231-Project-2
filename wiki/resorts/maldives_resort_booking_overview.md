# Maldives Resort Booking Overview

## Summary
Booking.com maintains a comprehensive directory of over 1,000 accommodations across the Maldives archipelago, organized by atoll regions and major islands. The platform surfaces popular resorts with guest ratings and serves as a current-availability snapshot for travelers.

## Key Facts
- Booking.com lists over 1,000 places to stay across the Maldives
- Major booking concentrations in Kaafu Atoll, Ari Atoll, North Malé Atoll, South Malé Atoll, Baa Atoll, Addu Atoll, and Gaafu Atoll
- Key island destinations include Malé, Hulhumale, Thoddoo, Maafushi, Fuvahmulah, Ukulhas, Thulusdhoo, Dhigurah, and Dhiffushi
- Popular surfaced properties include Furaveri Maldives, Sun Siyam Olhuveli, Niva Kurumba Maldives, and LUX* South Ari Atoll
- Directory organized by guest ratings and traveler demand metrics
- Highest concentration of bookings in most-visited atolls
