# Plastic Reduction in Resorts

## Summary
A sustainability initiative focused on eliminating single-use plastics from resort operations. Banyan Tree implemented resort-wide plastic reduction starting in 2018.

## Key Facts
- Banyan Tree began single-use plastic reduction efforts in 2018
- Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru and Dhawa Ihuru achieved a 75% reduction in total single-use plastic usage
- Plastic reduction is part of broader sustainability certification requirements
- The initiative reflects growing environmental standards in luxury hospitality
