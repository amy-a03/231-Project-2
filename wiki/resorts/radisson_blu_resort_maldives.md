# Radisson Blu Resort Maldives

## Summary
A resort located approximately 105 km from Velana International Airport, offering multiple transfer options to reach the property. It serves as a practical example of common transportation choices available to guests arriving in the Maldives.

## Key Facts
- Located 105 km from Velana International Airport
- Direct seaplane transfer takes approximately 30 minutes
- Alternative route: 20-minute domestic flight to Maamigili Airport followed by 15-minute speedboat ride
- Offers both seaplane and domestic flight-plus-speedboat transfer options
