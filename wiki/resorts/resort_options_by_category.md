# Resort Options by Category

## Summary
The Maldives accommodates various budgets with resort options ranging from ultra-luxury properties to relatively affordable mid-range facilities. Notable examples include luxury resorts like W Maldives and more accessible alternatives like Summer Island.

## Key Facts
- Kandolhu, W Maldives, and Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort represent higher-end options
- Summer Island and Paradise Island offer relatively more affordable resort experiences
- Resort accessibility and pricing vary significantly by location and distance from airport
- Some resorts remain luxury splurges while others are more budget-conscious
