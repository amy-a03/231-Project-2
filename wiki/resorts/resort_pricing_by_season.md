# Resort Pricing by Season

## Summary
Resort pricing fluctuates significantly throughout the year, with premium rates during high season and substantial discounts available during monsoon months.

## Key Facts
- High season (December-April) requires advance booking due to popularity and higher prices
- April marks the end of high season when better travel deals become available
- June is recommended for travelers seeking lower resort prices, especially in southern atolls
- August is popular despite wet-season conditions because resorts offer significant discounts
- May through October represents low season with reduced pricing
