# Resort Transfer Options

## Summary
Multiple transportation methods connect Malé airport to resort islands, each with different costs and travel times. Options range from local ferries to seaplanes, allowing travelers to balance budget constraints with convenience.

## Key Facts
- Local ferry is the cheapest but slowest transfer option
- Speedboat offers faster travel at moderate cost
- Seaplane is the fastest option but also the most expensive
- Domestic flights combined with speedboat transfers are available for distant islands
- Transfer method choice significantly impacts overall trip budget
