# Resort Transfers

## Summary
Pre-arranged transportation services coordinated by resorts to move guests from the airport to their destination properties across the atolls.

## Key Facts
- Transfers are typically arranged in advance by resorts
- Close resorts use speedboat transfers
- Distant resorts use seaplane transfers
- Transfer method varies based on resort distance from Velana International Airport
