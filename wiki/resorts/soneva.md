# Soneva

## Summary
Soneva is a world-leading sustainable luxury resort operator founded in 1995, operating multiple properties across the Maldives and Southeast Asia. The company is committed to net-positive hospitality with a business purpose beyond shareholder returns, measured through comprehensive Total Impact Assessment frameworks.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 1995 as a sustainable luxury resort operator
- Operates three resorts in the Maldives: Soneva Fushi, Soneva Jani, and Soneva Secret
- Uses Total Impact Assessment to measure direct and indirect social and environmental impacts including supply chain and guest travel
- Soneva Foundation Coral Restoration programme aims to outplant 150,000 coral fragments annually
- Focuses on sustainability initiatives in air quality, biodiversity, youth, and zero waste
- Member of the World Sustainable Hospitality Alliance
- Operates Soneva Kiri in Thailand as part of wider portfolio
