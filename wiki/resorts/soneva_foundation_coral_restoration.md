# Soneva Foundation Coral Restoration

## Summary
The Soneva Foundation operates a coral restoration programme in the Maldives dedicated to restoring reef systems through large-scale coral fragment outplanting efforts.

## Key Facts
- Annual target of 150,000 coral fragment outplants
- Focused on restoring reef systems in the Maldives
- Part of Soneva's net-positive hospitality commitment
- Addresses biodiversity conservation and coral ecosystem recovery
